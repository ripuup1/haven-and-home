## Frequently Asked Questions

### What bathroom accessories make a bathroom look expensive?

A refillable soap dispenser ($16), matching toothbrush holder ($14), and ceramic tissue box cover ($18) are the three accessories that make the biggest impact. Choose one consistent finish (matte black, brushed brass, or white ceramic) across all pieces. Coordinated accessories instantly make a bathroom look intentional and high-end.

### What color bathroom accessories are most popular?

Matte black and matte white are the most popular and versatile bathroom accessory finishes. Matte black adds contrast and works with white, gray, and marble surfaces. Matte white creates a clean, spa-like look. Brushed brass or gold adds warmth and pairs well with both. Choose one primary finish and stick with it for a cohesive look.

### How do you make a rental bathroom look luxury?

Focus on accessories you can take with you: a refillable soap dispenser, matching toothbrush holder, tissue box cover, and glass storage jars. Add spa-quality towels in white or neutral tones. These changes require zero modifications to the space and make an immediate visual impact for under $100 total.
