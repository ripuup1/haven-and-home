## Do Adhesive Shower Shelves Actually Hold?

**Adhesive shower shelves ($16 for a set of 2) hold up to 15 lbs each when installed correctly on smooth, non-porous surfaces. The key is cleaning the wall with rubbing alcohol first and waiting 24 hours before loading them.**

Adhesive shelves have gotten dramatically better in the last few years. The newer adhesive strips hold more weight than suction cups ever could, and they don't lose grip over time from steam and moisture. This set of two corner shelves holds shampoo, conditioner, and body wash without any drilling or hardware. The critical step most people skip: clean the tile with rubbing alcohol before applying, then wait a full 24 hours before putting anything on the shelf. That waiting period lets the adhesive cure fully. Skip it and the shelf falls within a week. Follow it and the shelf holds for years.

The number one reason shower caddies end up in the trash is rust. Chrome-plated caddies look shiny in the store but the plating chips within a few months and brown rust streaks start running down your shower wall. Look for caddies made from solid stainless steel, aluminum, or powder-coated steel. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### What type of shower caddy doesn't fall?

Tension pole shower caddies are the most secure because they're wedged between the floor and ceiling with spring pressure. Over-showerhead caddies are the second most reliable because they hook over the pipe rather than sticking to the wall. Adhesive shelves also hold well if you clean the surface with rubbing alcohol first and wait 24 hours before loading them.

### How do you keep a suction cup shower caddy from falling?

Clean the wall with rubbing alcohol before attaching, press firmly for 30 seconds, and wait 24 hours before adding weight. Even with perfect installation, suction cups lose grip over time from steam and temperature changes. If you're tired of reattaching suction cups, switch to an adhesive shelf or over-showerhead caddy for a permanent solution.

### What is the best shower caddy for a rental apartment?

The over-showerhead hanging caddy ($28) is the best option for renters because it requires zero installation, leaves no marks, and works in any shower type. Adhesive shelves are a close second since most modern adhesives remove cleanly. Avoid drilling into tile, which will cost you your security deposit.
