## Quick Tips for Choosing a Bathroom Mirror Cabinet

- **Measure your wall space first** -- check both width and depth, especially if you want a recessed cabinet (you need at least 3.5 inches of wall depth between studs)
- **Consider your mounting style** -- recessed looks more built-in and saves space, but surface mount is way easier to install and doesn't require cutting drywall
- **Match your hardware finish** -- a brushed nickel cabinet next to oil-rubbed bronze faucets will look off, so pay attention to the hinge and frame color
- **Check the shelf spacing** -- if you store tall bottles, look for adjustable shelves so nothing gets crammed in sideways
- **Don't forget the door swing** -- make sure the cabinet door won't hit a light fixture or wall sconce when it opens
