## Quick Tips for Organizing Cereal in Your Pantry

- Decant cereal as soon as you open the box. The sooner it's in an airtight container, the longer it stays fresh.
- Label containers with the expiration date from the original box, not just the cereal name.
- Store cereal containers at eye level or one shelf below. You're more likely to eat what you can see.
- Keep a small bin nearby for cereal bags that are almost empty. Consolidate rather than storing half-empty containers.
- If you have kids, put their cereal on a lower shelf in containers they can open themselves. It changes the morning routine.

Stale cereal is such a small annoyance that you almost forget how annoying it is until you fix it. Any of these containers will keep your cereal fresh and your pantry looking organized. Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
