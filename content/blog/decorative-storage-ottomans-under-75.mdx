## Quick Tips for Choosing a Storage Ottoman

- **Match the height to your sofa.** A footrest should sit within a few inches of your sofa seat height — typically 16-18 inches is the sweet spot for standard sofas.
- **Consider the lid mechanism.** Hinged lids stay attached and open hands-free; removable lids offer more flexibility but can get misplaced.
- **Use a tray on top.** A decorative tray on a square ottoman makes it work as a coffee table surface and keeps items from sliding.
- **Think about the finish.** Velvet looks luxurious but shows impressions; linen is more forgiving; performance fabric is best if you have pets or kids.
- **Check the interior dimensions, not just the exterior.** The frame and padding take up space — a 20-inch exterior ottoman might only give you 15 inches of usable interior width.
