## Quick Tips for Keeping Your Couch Area Tidy

- **One remote, one spot.** If you have multiple remotes, keep them all in the same pocket or caddy. The system only works if everyone puts them back.
- **Empty it weekly.** Armrest caddies become junk drawers if you don't clear out receipts, snack wrappers, and random stuff that accumulates.
- **Match the material.** Linen caddies look best on fabric sofas. Faux-leather options blend better with leather recliners. The right match makes it look intentional, not like an afterthought.
