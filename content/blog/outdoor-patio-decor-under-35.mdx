## 4. Small Planter Pots (Set of 5)

A cluster of small planters along the edge of your patio or grouped on a table adds so much life to the space. Grab a basil leaf for your drink while you're sitting out there. Very main character behavior.

Make sure whatever pots you choose have drainage holes. Plants sitting in water after a rainstorm will turn into a mushy mess faster than you'd expect.

## 5. Do You Really Need an Outdoor Rug?

**Yes. An Outdoor Patio Area Rug ($34, 14,800+ reviews) defines your seating area and makes the whole space feel like a room instead of just a slab. Modern outdoor rugs handle rain and sun, and they're easy to clean -- just hose them off.**

This is the one people always skip because they don't realize outdoor rugs exist, or they assume they'll be ugly. Modern outdoor rugs are actually gorgeous. They come in great patterns, they're made to handle rain and sun, and they define your seating area in a way that makes the whole space feel like a room instead of just a slab.
