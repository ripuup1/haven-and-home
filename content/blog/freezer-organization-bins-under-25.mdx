## Quick Tips for Freezer Organization That Actually Lasts

- **Label everything before it goes in.** A piece of masking tape and a marker takes five seconds but saves you from mystery meals weeks later.
- **Organize by meal category**, not by ingredient type. A "Tuesday dinner" bin beats a "proteins" bin when you're tired at 5pm.
- **Use the first-in, first-out rule.** New groceries go behind older ones. Works for the freezer just as well as the pantry.
- **Do a monthly audit.** Set a phone reminder. A quick 10-minute check each month prevents the dreaded 2-year-old freezer burn situation.
- **Don't overfill the bins.** Leave a few inches of clearance so you can actually pull them out easily when loaded.
