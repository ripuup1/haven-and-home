## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best kitchen drawer organizer on Amazon?

The best kitchen drawer organizer depends on the drawer. For utensils, an expandable bamboo tray ($18) fits any width and has deep compartments. For silverware, a five-compartment organizer with a nonslip base ($14) keeps everything separated. For the junk drawer, a modular interlocking set ($15 for 8 pieces) lets you customize compartments for your specific collection of stuff.

### How do you organize a messy kitchen drawer?

Empty the entire drawer first. Throw away anything broken, expired, or unidentifiable. Group similar items together and measure your drawer. Buy an organizer that fits (expandable styles work for non-standard widths). Place items back in groups with the most-used items in the most accessible spots. The whole process takes about 15 minutes per drawer.

### Are bamboo drawer organizers better than plastic?

Bamboo drawer organizers look more polished, don't crack or discolor over time, and are more environmentally friendly than plastic. They cost slightly more but last significantly longer. Plastic is fine for hidden drawers like the junk drawer, but bamboo is worth the upgrade for drawers you open frequently or that guests might see.
