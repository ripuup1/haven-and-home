## Quick Tips for Organizing Scarves and Accessories

- Group by type first (scarves together, belts together), then by color within each group
- Store seasonal accessories in a separate area so your daily picks are front and center
- Use the back of your closet door for pocket organizers — it is free real estate you are probably wasting
- Lightweight scarves do best on ring or slot hangers; heavy belts need hook-style organizers
- Take everything out once a season and donate what you have not worn — a half-empty organizer works better than an overstuffed one
