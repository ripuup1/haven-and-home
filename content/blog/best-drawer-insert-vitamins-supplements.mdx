Does your vitamin situation look like a junk drawer that got into an argument with a pharmacy? Bottles tipped over, half-empty blister packs stuffed behind the magnesium, a random melatonin tablet rolling free every time you open the cabinet? You're not disorganized — you just don't have the right system yet.

A good vitamin drawer insert is the simplest upgrade you can make to your morning routine. Spend $15 to $35 once, and suddenly taking your supplements goes from a daily scavenger hunt to a two-second grab-and-go. Here's everything you need to know to pick the right one.

## What to Look For in a Vitamin Drawer Insert

Before you buy, keep these criteria in mind:

- **Depth matters more than width.** Supplement bottles are tall. Look for inserts with compartments at least 3 inches deep, or ones designed to hold bottles upright.
- **Expandability is a game-changer.** An insert that adjusts to different drawer widths means you're not stuck replacing it when you move or rearrange.
- **Clear vs. bamboo.** Clear acrylic lets you see everything at a glance. Bamboo looks better on a countertop if you ever want to pull it out and display it.
- **Lids and latches.** If you have small kids or pets, a locking insert is worth the premium.
- **Weekly pill organizers.** If you take multiple supplements daily, an integrated weekly pill tray built into a drawer insert saves enormous time — you fill it once on Sunday and you're done.

## How to Choose

Start by measuring your drawer — width, depth, and height clearance — before you buy anything. Most kitchen drawers run 14 to 20 inches wide and 3 to 4 inches deep, while bathroom drawers are often narrower and shallower. If you take more than four supplements, go with an organizer that holds bottles upright rather than a flat tray. If you have kids, get the locking version without debate. And if you're genuinely unsure whether a dedicated vitamin drawer will stick as a habit, start with the budget clear acrylic tray. If you're still using it three months from now (you will be), upgrade to bamboo.
