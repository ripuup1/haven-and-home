## Quick Tips for Choosing a Bedside Lamp With USB Charging

- **USB-A vs. USB-C matters.** Most older cables use USB-A. Most newer phones use USB-C. Check what you actually need before choosing.
- **Consider your bulb.** Many of these lamps are LED-compatible but ship without a bulb. Check the product listing for whether a bulb is included.
- **Three-way dimming is worth it.** A lamp with only an on/off switch becomes annoying very quickly in a bedroom. Three brightness levels covers every situation from night-light to reading.
- **Measure your nightstand.** A lamp base that is too wide for your nightstand looks awkward. Most of these have bases under 6 inches in diameter, but check the specs for your specific situation.
- **One lamp or two?** If you and a partner both use the nightstand, look for sets of two. Several of these lamps are available in matching pairs, often at a per-lamp price lower than buying individually.
