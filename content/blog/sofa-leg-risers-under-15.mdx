One of the sneakiest tricks for adding storage to a small living room costs less than a latte. Furniture risers — those unassuming little blocks that go under sofa legs — lift your couch just enough to slide flat bins, shoe boxes, or storage bags underneath. Suddenly you have an invisible storage zone that didn't exist before.

Beyond the storage benefit, raising your sofa a few inches also makes a room feel more open and airy. Low-slung furniture can make ceilings feel lower; give everything a little lift and the whole space breathes differently. It's one of those $10-$15 changes that has an outsized visual impact on a room.

## What to Store Under a Raised Sofa

Once you've lifted your sofa 3-6 in., here's what fits beautifully underneath:

- **Flat storage bins:** Target, IKEA, and Amazon all sell shallow bins sized specifically for under-bed storage that slide under a raised sofa perfectly.
- **Rolled blankets:** Roll extra throw blankets and tuck them in a row — it's surprisingly easy to grab one without anyone ever seeing the storage zone.
- **Board games and puzzles:** Flat game boxes fit perfectly under a 4 in. raised sofa and keep them out of closets.
- **Kids' toys:** A shallow bin under the sofa becomes a quick toy cleanup zone for the living room.
- **Shoes:** In smaller homes, under-sofa storage is one of the best places to keep extra shoes in their original boxes.

## Quick Tips for Installing Sofa Leg Risers

- **Measure your leg diameter first:** Check whether your sofa has round legs, square legs, or a flat base — this determines which riser style fits.
- **Use all legs:** Always put risers under every leg, not just the front or back ones. Uneven heights stress the sofa frame.
- **Stack for more height:** Most risers are designed to stack — two 2 in. risers give you 4 in. of lift, three give you 6 in.
- **Test stability before loading:** Give the raised sofa a gentle push before sitting on it to confirm the risers are seated correctly.
- **Match your bins to the lift height:** Measure the gap once installed and buy under-bed bins that are about half an inch shorter for easy sliding.
