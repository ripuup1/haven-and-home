## Quick Tips

- Most herbs need 6-8 hours of sunlight daily. A south-facing window is ideal, but east or west-facing works too.
- Water when the top inch of soil feels dry. Overwatering kills more indoor herbs than underwatering.
- Harvest from the top of the plant and never cut more than a third at once. This encourages bushy growth instead of leggy, sparse plants.
