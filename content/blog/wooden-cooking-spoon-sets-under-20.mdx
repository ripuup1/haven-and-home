## Quick Tips for Wooden Cooking Spoons

- Hand wash and dry immediately — soaking in water is the fastest way to crack wooden utensils
- Condition your wooden spoons with a light coat of food-grade mineral oil every few months to prevent drying
- Store them upright in a holder, not flat in a drawer, to prevent warping
- Avoid dishwashers — the heat and prolonged moisture will split even good teak eventually
- Darker teak means denser grain — a good sign for durability
