## Quick Tips for Choosing a C-Shaped Side Table

- **Measure your sofa arm height first.** The table's inner clearance needs to be taller than your sofa arm or it won't slide over cleanly. Most sofa arms are 22-26 inches, so check before you buy.
- **Consider your flooring.** On hardwood, look for tables with rubber feet or felt pads so you don't scratch the floor when sliding it around.
- **Think about weight capacity.** Most C-tables hold 15-25 lbs, which is plenty for drinks and snacks but not enough for heavy equipment. If you want to use it as a laptop desk, go for a sturdier option like the HOOBRO.
