Swap out a builder-grade chrome towel bar for a brushed gold one and suddenly the room looks like you spent thousands on a renovation. ## Quick Tips

- Brushed gold hides fingerprints and water spots better than polished gold
- SUS304 stainless steel with PVD coating is the most durable finish for humid bathrooms
- Use painter's tape and a level when mounting to ensure all pieces align at the same height
- Match your gold hardware to your faucet finish — brushed gold hardware with a polished gold faucet will look mismatched
