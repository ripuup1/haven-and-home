## Quick Tips for Getting the Most from Your Sink Mat

- **Measure your sink basin before ordering.** Measure the flat bottom of your sink, not the full interior width. Most mat sizing refers to the flat bottom dimensions.
- **Silicone mats are dishwasher safe.** If yours looks dingy, a dishwasher cycle will restore it. Avoid abrasive scrubbers which can leave micro-scratches.
- **Pull it out when soaking dishes.** Mats are not designed to sit under standing water for hours. Pull it out before soaking pots, and your mat will last much longer.
- **Black mats hide water spots.** If you live in a hard-water area, a dark-colored mat shows much less spotting than white or gray versions.
- **Replace every two to three years.** Even high-quality silicone mats eventually develop a surface texture that holds bacteria. When the surface feels rough rather than smooth, it is time for a new one.

One good sink mat is one of those small home upgrades that pays off daily in chips prevented, scratches avoided, and dishes that last longer than they otherwise would.
