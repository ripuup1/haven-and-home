## Quick Tips for Hanging Basket Success

- Water in the morning so leaves dry before evening — wet foliage overnight invites fungal problems.
- Feed with a liquid fertilizer every two weeks once plants are established; hanging baskets burn through nutrients faster than ground plantings.
- Rotate baskets a quarter turn weekly if one side gets more sun — this prevents lopsided growth.
- Check hook and bracket weight ratings before planting; a 12-inch basket full of wet soil can weigh 15-20 pounds.
