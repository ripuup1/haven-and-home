## Quick Tips for the Ultimate Rainy Day Setup

- Stage your cozy corner the night before a rainy forecast so you wake up ready
- Layer your chunky knit blanket with the wearable hoodie for maximum warmth
- Set the mug warmer on your coffee table so refills stay hot without trips to the kitchen
- Light the petrichor candle 30 minutes before you settle in so the scent fills the room
- Queue up your show or book beforehand — the less you have to move once you are cozy, the better

Rainy days are not something to endure. With the right setup, they become the best days of the week. Found your favorites? Pin this for later so you are ready for the next grey afternoon!
