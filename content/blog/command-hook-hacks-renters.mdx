### How much weight can adhesive hooks actually hold?
Modern adhesive hooks hold between 10-16 pounds each, depending on the model. Gallery Wall Hanging Strips hold up to 16 lbs per set. Heavy-Duty Utility Hooks hold 13 lbs each. ### Do adhesive hooks work on textured walls?
Adhesive hooks work best on smooth surfaces like painted drywall, tile, glass, and laminate. They don't stick well to textured, unpainted, or porous surfaces like brick or stucco. If your walls are lightly textured, the heavy-duty utility hooks ($12/6-pack) have the strongest adhesive and are your best bet.
