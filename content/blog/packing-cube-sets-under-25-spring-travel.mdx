Spring packing is tricky because you need layers. A light jacket, a few warmer pieces for evenings, and your warm-weather staples all have to coexist. A good cube set lets you segment by category or by day-of-outfit so you're not re-packing every time you move hotels.

## Quick Tips for Packing Cubes

- Roll clothes before placing them in cubes — you'll fit significantly more and wrinkles are reduced
- Assign one cube per category: tops, bottoms, underwear/socks, and one for layering pieces
- Pack your cubes vertically in your suitcase like files in a drawer so you can see everything at once
- Use the smallest cube for your most-needed items (tomorrow's outfit) so it's easy to grab at the hotel
- Color-code your set if traveling with a partner — it's easier than checking tags when you're tired
