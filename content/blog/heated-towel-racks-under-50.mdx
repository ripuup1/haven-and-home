## Quick Tips for Heated Towel Racks

- Use a timer outlet ($8 on Amazon) to turn your warmer on 15 minutes before your usual shower time — warm towels waiting for you every morning
- Heated racks also work amazingly for drying delicates that can't go in the dryer
- Keep your rack at least 6 inches from the wall to allow air circulation behind towels
- In humid bathrooms, a heated rack actually helps prevent that musty towel smell by keeping fabric dry between uses
