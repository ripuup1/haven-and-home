## Quick Tips for Organizing Container Lids

- **Match lids to containers first.** Before organizing, pull everything out and match each lid to its container. Toss any orphan lids that don't fit anything you own.
- **Store lids separately from containers.** Nesting containers inside each other saves space. Storing lids in a separate organizer means you're not unstacking everything just to find one lid.
- **Standardize your container brand.** If you use one brand (Rubbermaid, Pyrex, etc.), all the lids are interchangeable within sizes. Mixing brands is what creates the chaos.
