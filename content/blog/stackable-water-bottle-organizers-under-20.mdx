## Quick Tips for an Organized Bottle Cabinet

- **Declutter first.** Before you buy any organizer, toss mismatched lids and bottles you have not used in six months. Organization products work best in a streamlined collection.
- **Measure your cabinet depth.** Some organizers are wider than a standard cabinet is deep. Check dimensions before ordering.
- **Separate lids.** Add a small bin or drawer divider just for lids — this is the real secret to a functional bottle cabinet.
- **Stack by size.** Put the tallest bottles in the back or bottom tier, shorter ones up front. It makes grabbing the right bottle effortless.
- **Clean before organizing.** Wipe down the shelf and the organizer before loading — it is much easier to do it when everything is empty.
