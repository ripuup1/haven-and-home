## Quick Tips for Mounting Hair Dryer Holders

- Always clean the wall surface with rubbing alcohol before applying adhesive — oils and dust are the #1 reason holders fall off
- Mount your holder near an outlet so the cord reaches without stretching across the counter
- If you share a bathroom, grab two — one per person keeps the peace and the organization intact
- For textured walls or wallpaper, skip adhesive and go with screw-mount options every time
