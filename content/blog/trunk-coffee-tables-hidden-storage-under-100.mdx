## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Trunk Coffee Table

- **Measure before you buy.** The standard rule is that your coffee table should be two-thirds the length of your sofa. For a 90-inch sofa, that's a 60-inch table. Most of these options run 36 to 43 inches, which works for loveseats and smaller sofas.
- **Use the hidden compartment for things you actually use.** If you stash things in there and forget about them, you've just created a chaos drawer at knee height. Reserve it for throw blankets in winter or extra napkins and outdoor cushions in summer.
- **Don't overfill the top.** A lift-top table works best when the surface is mostly clear. One tray with a candle and a stack of coasters is all you need.
- **Felt pads are your friend.** Most of these come with felt pads for the feet, but if yours doesn't, pick up a pack from the dollar store. Hardwood floors and table legs are not a good combination.
- **The lower shelf is display space, not junk space.** A basket, a stack of oversized books, or a small plant will make the whole table look intentional.
