## Quick Tips for Hanging Patio Umbrella String Lights

- **Wrap lights around the ribs from the hub outward** for an even, spoke-like pattern — it's the look you see in professional outdoor spaces.
- **Drape excess length around the outer edge of the canopy** for a full, lush glow.
- **Solar lights need at least 6 hours of direct sun during the day** to run all evening — make sure your umbrella spot gets good afternoon light.
- **Use S-hooks or binder clips** to secure longer string lights to the umbrella poles without damaging the fabric.
- **Layer globe string lights with fairy string lights** for a layered, magazine-worthy effect — the different bulb sizes create depth.
