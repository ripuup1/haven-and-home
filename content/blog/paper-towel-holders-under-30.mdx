## Quick Tips for Choosing a Paper Towel Holder

- If you tear paper towels one-handed while cooking, prioritize a weighted base or wall/cabinet mount over a lightweight standing holder.
- Matte black and brushed stainless are the most versatile finishes that won't clash with your other kitchen hardware.
- Under-cabinet mounts are the best option for small kitchens where every inch of counter space matters.
- Self-adhesive mounts work great on smooth surfaces but struggle with textured materials. When in doubt, use the included screws.
- Consider where you reach for paper towels most often (near the stove, near the sink) and mount accordingly.
