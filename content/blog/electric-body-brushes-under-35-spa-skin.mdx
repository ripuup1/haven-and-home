## Quick Tips for Using an Electric Body Brush

- Start with the softest brush head and work your way up — over-exfoliating can irritate skin, especially the first week
- Use your brush on damp skin with body wash or soap for the best lather and gentlest exfoliation
- Clean brush heads weekly by rinsing thoroughly and letting them air dry outside the shower
- Replace brush heads every 2-3 months, or sooner if bristles start to flatten or fray
- Avoid using on sunburned, broken, or freshly shaved skin — give it at least 24 hours
