## What Makes a Mini Waffle Maker Worth Buying?

**The two things that actually matter are heating speed and nonstick quality.** A good mini waffle maker heats up in under 3 minutes and releases the waffle cleanly without you having to pry it out with a fork. Most picks under $20 nail both of these.

One thing to know going in: mini waffle makers make small waffles — typically 4 to 5 in. across. That's intentional. If you want full-size Belgian waffles, you need a different appliance. But for a quick personal portion, they're perfect.

## Quick Tips for Getting the Best Results from a Mini Waffle Maker

- Let the iron fully preheat before pouring in batter — most models have an indicator light that tells you when it's ready
- A very light spray of cooking spray on the nonstick plates extends their life and helps with release, even though the manufacturer says you don't need it
- For crispy waffles, let them sit on a wire rack for 1-2 minutes after cooking rather than stacking them — stacking creates steam that softens the exterior
- Mini waffle irons work great with boxed pancake mix — just thin it slightly and you're good to go
- For chaffles, use 1 egg and 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella — pour it in, close the lid, and cook for about 3-4 minutes
