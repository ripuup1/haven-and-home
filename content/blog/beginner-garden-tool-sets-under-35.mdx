## Quick Tips for Beginner Gardeners

- **Start small** -- a few containers or a single raised bed is plenty for your first season; you can always expand next year
- **Clean your tools after each use** -- a quick wipe-down prevents rust and keeps blades sharp longer
- **Invest in good gloves** -- cheap gloves rip within a week; look for reinforced fingertips and a snug fit
- **Keep a kneeling pad handy** -- your knees will hurt way sooner than you think, especially on concrete or packed soil
- **Don't skip the pruning shears** -- even beginners need them for deadheading flowers, trimming herbs, and cutting back overgrowth
