## Quick Tips for Under-Sink Organization

- **Line the cabinet floor** with a waterproof mat or shelf liner — leaks happen and this protects the wood
- **Store spray bottles with nozzles turned to the side** so they don't accidentally get pressed and leak
- **Keep daily cleaners in front** and specialty products (oven cleaner, drain opener) in the back
- **Use a tension rod** across the top of the cabinet to hang spray bottles by their triggers — instant extra space
- **Check for leaks monthly** and move items away from pipe connections where drips are most likely
