Spring entertaining is the season of pitchers on tables — lemonade at the porch gathering, infused water at the brunch, sangria at the dinner party. A good pitcher set does more for your table presentation than almost any other single item, and the good news is that you do not need to spend much. The sets below all come in under $25 and look significantly more expensive than they are.

The choice between glass, acrylic, and infuser-style pitchers comes down to where and how you will use them. Glass looks the best on a dining table and does not affect flavor, but it breaks if it falls on a patio. Acrylic is nearly indestructible for outdoor use and looks like glass from a few feet away, but it can scratch over time. Infuser pitchers have a built-in core for adding fruit, herbs, or tea bags directly to the water, which is both functional and gorgeous as a centerpiece. Here is what is worth buying for spring.

Whether you are hosting a casual backyard cookout or a sit-down brunch, one of these pitcher sets will make your table look intentional and put-together without breaking the budget.

## Quick Tips for Spring Entertaining Drinks

- Make lemonade concentrate ahead of time and keep it in a mason jar in the fridge — dilute to taste when filling pitchers so you only make what you need
- Add herb sprigs (rosemary, basil, mint) to pitchers 30 minutes before guests arrive for subtle flavor without overpowering
- Freeze fruit in ice cube trays to use as ice cubes that add flavor instead of diluting your drinks
- Place pitchers in the shade — direct sunlight heats drinks surprisingly fast and wilts fresh garnishes
