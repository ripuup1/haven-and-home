## Quick Tips for Styling an Outdoor Bar Cart

- **Keep the top shelf for active use.** The shaker, the tray, the current bottle you're pouring from. Everything else goes on lower shelves.
- **Group small items in a small bowl or dish.** Wine stoppers, cocktail picks, and cocktail napkins look intentional in a bowl rather than scattered across the shelf.
- **A small potted succulent or herb in a waterproof pot adds life without requiring maintenance.** Rosemary is especially good next to a bar cart because it smells wonderful when you brush against it.
- **Choose a cart with locking wheels** so it doesn't roll away on an uneven deck surface.
- **Keep a small ice bucket nearby** instead of making guests go inside for ice. A fabric-lined bucket with a lid keeps ice cold for hours and looks great on the lower cart shelf.
