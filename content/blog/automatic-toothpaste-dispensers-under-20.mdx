## Quick Tips for Toothpaste Dispensers

- Clean the dispenser nozzle once a week with a damp cloth to prevent dried paste from clogging the opening
- When loading a new tube, squeeze out any air first for smoother, more consistent dispensing
- Mount the dispenser at eye level for adults, or lower near the sink for kids to reach independently
- If using adhesive mounts, let the adhesive cure for 24 hours before loading a full toothpaste tube — the weight matters
