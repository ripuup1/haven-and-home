## Quick Tips

- **Skip light velvet if you have pets or kids** — cream and blush look amazing in photos and terrible after one weekend of real life.
- **Check the hinge type before ordering** — soft-close or safety hinges add maybe $5 to the price and prevent finger injuries and door-slam noise.
- **Measure interior depth, not just outside dimensions** — some "storage" ottomans have shallow trays that hold almost nothing.
- **Velvet pile attracts lint** — keep a sticky lint roller or a fabric brush within reach if you go with darker colors.
- **Wipe spills immediately** — even stain-resistant velvet stains if you let wine sit overnight.
