If you've ever balanced your phone on the edge of the tub, propped it against a shampoo bottle, or just accepted that you'll miss the end of the podcast when you shower, a waterproof phone holder fixes all of that for under $20. These wall-mounted cases seal your phone behind a clear, touch-sensitive screen so you can control music, follow recipes, or watch videos without any water damage risk.

The good ones stick to tile, glass, and mirrors with strong adhesive mounts — no drilling required. Most fit phones up to 6.8 inches, which covers everything from an iPhone SE to a Galaxy S24 Ultra. The key differences come down to rotation, anti-fog performance, and how well the touchscreen actually works through the case. Here are five that deliver.

## Quick Tips for Shower Phone Holders

- Clean the mounting surface with rubbing alcohol before applying the adhesive for the strongest bond
- Mount the holder at chest height and slightly off-center from the shower head to avoid direct water streams
- Let the adhesive cure for 24 hours before placing your phone in the holder
- If you use a thick phone case, measure your phone with the case on to make sure it fits the holder's size limit
