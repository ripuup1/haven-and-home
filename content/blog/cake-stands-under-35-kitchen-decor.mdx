## Quick Tips for Styling a Cake Stand as Decor

- Keep it visible. A cake stand tucked in a corner doesn't do its job. Place it where you'll see it daily.
- Change what's on it seasonally. Pumpkins in fall, pinecones in winter, lemons in summer.
- Odd numbers look better. Three items on a stand looks more styled than two or four.
- Layer heights when possible. A candle next to stacked books on a stand creates visual interest.
- Don't overthink it. Even a single beautiful bowl of fruit on a cake stand elevates a kitchen counter.
