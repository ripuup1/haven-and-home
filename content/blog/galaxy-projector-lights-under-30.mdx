## Quick Tips for Using Galaxy Projectors in Your Bedroom

- Position the projector 3-5 feet from the ceiling for the sharpest star projection — too close and the image is small, too far and it gets blurry
- Set a sleep timer so the projector turns off after you fall asleep instead of running all night
- Use the lowest brightness setting for sleep — bright projections look impressive but can actually keep you awake
- Combine the projector with blackout curtains for the most dramatic effect during daytime naps
- Keep the projector dust-free — dust on the lens creates blurry spots in the projection
