## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are waffle towels as absorbent as regular towels?

Yes — waffle weave towels are highly absorbent despite being lighter than traditional terry cloth. The grid texture increases surface area contact with skin, which actually makes them faster to dry with. They also release moisture more quickly, so they dry between uses faster than a thick terry towel.

### What size waffle towels do I need for a bath towel replacement?

Look for bath towels that are at least 27 x 52 inches. Premium options are 30 x 56 inches. Waffle towels run slightly lighter in weight so the larger size ensures full coverage even though the towel is thinner than a terry alternative.

### Do waffle towels get softer over time?

Yes. Linen-cotton waffle towels in particular get noticeably softer with each wash. Pre-washed versions are softer out of the box. Avoid fabric softener — it coats the fibers and reduces absorbency over time. Line drying or a low-heat tumble dry keeps them in best shape.
