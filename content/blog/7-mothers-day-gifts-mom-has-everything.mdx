## Quick Tips

- Order by Thursday for standard Amazon Prime delivery before Mother's Day Sunday
- If you're combining gifts, pair the ring dish + candle set for a dresser refresh combo that looks like you spent more than you did
- Include a handwritten note inside the package — it's the part she'll actually keep
- For moms with scent sensitivities, stick to unscented or lightly scented options like the herb garden or the bamboo tray
- Don't overthink it — a useful gift wrapped with intention beats a fancy gift chosen by committee

Mother's Day doesn't require an extravagant gesture. It requires paying attention. Any of the seven picks above show that you noticed something specific about her — and that's worth more than the price tag.
