## Quick Tips for a Cinco de Mayo Tablescape

- Layer from the bottom up: table cover or skirt first, then runners, then centerpieces, then place settings
- Use mismatched colorful plates and glasses — the more color, the more authentic it feels
- Add small bowls of limes, peppers, or dried flowers for natural texture
- String papel picado banners above the table for overhead color
- Set out small terracotta pots with succulents as take-home favors

Your Cinco de Mayo table should feel joyful and abundant, not precious. These are all affordable enough to use, enjoy, and not stress over. Found your favorites? Pin this for later so you are ready when May rolls around!
