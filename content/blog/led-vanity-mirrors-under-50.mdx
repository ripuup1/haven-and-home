## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your LED Mirror

- **Match your lighting mode to your plans.** Use the daylight setting when doing makeup you'll wear outside, and warm light for evening or event looks.
- **Height matters more than you think.** Position your mirror so it's roughly at eye level. Too low or too high changes how light falls across your face.
- **Keep it close for detail work.** For eyebrows and eyeliner, position the mirror about 12 in. from your face. Farther back is fine for overall looks.
- **Wipe the mirror weekly.** Makeup mist and humidity create a film that dulls the light quality. A quick wipe with a microfiber cloth makes a big difference.
- **Go cordless if you travel regularly.** The slight trade-off in brightness is worth the convenience of not hunting for outlets.
