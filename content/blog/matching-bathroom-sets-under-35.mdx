## Quick Tips for Choosing a Bathroom Accessory Set

- **Match the set's finish to your existing hardware.** Chrome faucets look best with chrome or brushed nickel accessories. Matte black faucets pair naturally with matte black sets.
- **Count your actual counter items before buying.** If you use lotion, cotton balls, and a cup, get a 6-piece set. If you only use hand soap, a 2-piece is plenty.
- **Check the pump mechanism reviews.** This is the piece that fails most often — look for reviews specifically mentioning the pump works smoothly and doesn't stick.
- **Ceramic is easier to clean than resin.** Both look great, but ceramic wipes down without absorbing odors or discoloration over time.
- **Don't overlook the tumbler.** A tumbler that's awkward to grab or too heavy when full is surprisingly annoying to use daily.
