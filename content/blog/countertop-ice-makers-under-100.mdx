These compact machines produce ice in as little as 6 minutes, hold enough for a full day of drinks, and take up about as much space as a coffee maker. The best part? You don't need a water line. Just fill the reservoir, plug it in, and you're making ice.

## What's the Best Portable Ice Maker for Everyday Home Use?

**For everyday home use, a machine that makes 26 lbs of ice per day and has a 9-piece cycle every 6 minutes is your target. That's the sweet spot — enough ice to keep up with a household of 4 without babysitting it constantly.**

The bullet-ice style (small, cylindrical, soft) is what almost all of these make, and honestly most people prefer it to the hard cubed ice from their freezer. It chills drinks faster and doesn't shatter when you bite it. For cocktails, iced coffee, or just a water bottle on a hot day, bullet ice is legitimately better.

## Quick Tips for Getting the Most from Your Ice Maker

- Use filtered water if your tap water has a strong taste — the ice will taste better
- Run the self-cleaning cycle once a month, or every 2 weeks in summer
- Empty the reservoir if you're not using it for more than 24 hours to prevent bacterial growth
- Keep the machine in a spot with at least 6 inches of clearance on the sides for ventilation
- The ice melts and recycles back through — so you won't waste ice if you don't use it immediately
