## Quick Tips

- Measure your vanity height before buying — standard seat height is 18–20 inches, but some vanities run taller
- A stool with a footrest bar or lower rung is more comfortable for longer sessions
- For small spaces, choose a stool that tucks completely under the vanity when not in use
- Velvet shows lint easily — keep a lint roller nearby or opt for a tightly woven fabric
- Waterproof or wipeable seats are worth the upgrade if you use skincare products at your vanity
- Rotate the stool 90° occasionally to prevent uneven wear on upholstered seats

Your vanity deserves to be the best corner of your bedroom — and the right stool makes that possible without a major investment. Any of these picks will bring that hotel-suite energy home. Browse, compare, and let the morning routine upgrade itself.
