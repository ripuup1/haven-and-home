Open the cabinet under your bathroom vanity right now. If it looks anything like mine did six months ago, there are half-empty bottles of shampoo behind a tangled pile of hair tools next to a collection of hotel lotion samples you'll never use. The fix isn't complicated, but it does require the right organizers for the weird dimensions of vanity cabinets.
