## Quick Tips for Making Hanging Shelves Work in Any Closet

- **Measure your closet rod height before you order** — most hanging organizers need about 55-65 inches of vertical clearance below the rod to hang without touching the floor.
- **Group by category on each shelf**: one for T-shirts, one for jeans, one for sweaters — you'll be amazed how much calmer a categorized closet feels.
- **Use the top shelf for things you grab less often** and the eye-level shelves for everyday items.
- **Don't overload** — the magic of these organizers is easy access. If you cram them full, you lose that benefit fast.
- **Consider two organizers side by side** for a walk-in closet — it creates a wall of organized storage that looks genuinely intentional.
