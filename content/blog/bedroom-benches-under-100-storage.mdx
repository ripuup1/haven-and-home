## Quick Tips for Choosing and Styling a Bedroom Bench

- **Match the bench width to the bed width.** A full or queen bed looks balanced with a 40 to 45-inch bench. A king bed needs 48 inches or wider to not look proportionally small. When in doubt, go wider.
- **Consider the seat height relative to your mattress.** The bench should be within 2 to 3 inches of your mattress height for comfortable sitting. Most standard benches are 17 to 19 inches tall; measure your mattress height before ordering.
- **Leave 18 to 24 inches between the bench and the bed footboard.** This is the minimum clearance for walking past without sidling. If your bedroom is compact, prioritize a narrower bench over a wider one.
- **Use the storage seasonally.** A bedroom bench storage cavity is the perfect place for the heavy wool blanket in summer and the lighter cotton quilt in winter. Rotating seasonally keeps both accessible without taking closet space.
- **Fluff the cushion when it flattens.** Velvet and fabric cushions compress over time. Remove any inner cushion insert, give it a gentle shake and air out, and it will recover. Spot-clean velvet with a dry cloth — avoid water on velvet surfaces.
