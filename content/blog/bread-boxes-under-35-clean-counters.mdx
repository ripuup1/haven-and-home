A proper bread box lives in the sweet spot, creating a slightly humid micro-environment that keeps your loaf fresh for two to four days while also giving your kitchen counter a clean, purposeful look.

The bread boxes below all hit that perfect balance of function and style, and every single one is under $35. Most include a bamboo lid that doubles as a cutting board, which is a clever detail that makes daily bread slicing genuinely easier. Here are six worth putting on your counter.

## Quick Tips for Getting the Most from Your Bread Box

- Store bread in the box without the original plastic bag so the bamboo lid can help regulate moisture
- Wipe the inside of the box down once a week to prevent crumbs from attracting moisture and mold
- If your kitchen gets very warm, adding a small piece of dry paper towel inside the box absorbs excess humidity
- Homemade bread with no preservatives stays fresh for 2 to 3 days in the box, store-bought for 4 to 5
