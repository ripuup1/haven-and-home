## Quick Tips for Getting the Most from Shower Steamers and Bath Bombs

- Place shower steamers near the drain or in a corner — not directly under the water stream. Too much direct water dissolves them in 60 seconds.
- Store unused shower steamers in an airtight container or zip bag in a cool, dry spot. Bathroom humidity will degrade them over weeks.
- For bath bombs: run the tub halfway, add the bomb, then finish filling to disperse the fizz and fragrance evenly.
- If you have a large tub, use 1.5 to 2 bath bombs for full effect — a single bomb in a deep tub can get diluted quickly.
