## Quick Tips for Getting the Most From Your Shower Filter

- Let your shower run for 30 seconds before stepping in to flush the filter and activate the filtration media
- Replace cartridges on schedule — an expired filter can actually release trapped contaminants back into the water
- Pair a standard filter with essential oil shower steamers placed on the shower floor for true aromatherapy
- If you have very hard water, test your water first — some filters handle specific minerals better than others
- Vitamin C filters are best for chlorine and chloramine, while carbon filters are better for a wider range of contaminants
