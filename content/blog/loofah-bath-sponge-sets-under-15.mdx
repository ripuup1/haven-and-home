## Quick Tips for Getting the Most From Your Loofah

- Replace mesh poufs every 4-6 weeks and natural loofahs every 3-4 weeks to prevent bacteria buildup
- Rinse thoroughly after each use and hang it where air circulates — never leave it sitting in a puddle
- If your loofah starts to smell musty, it is time to toss it no matter how new it is
- Use about a quarter-sized amount of body wash — a good loofah stretches product way further than your hands
- Alternate between gentle and exfoliating loofahs if you use one daily, to avoid over-scrubbing
