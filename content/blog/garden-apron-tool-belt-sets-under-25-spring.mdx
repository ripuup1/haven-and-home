A garden apron solves all three problems. Your tools are always in the same pocket, your hands can go from dirty to phone in two seconds with a quick wipe, and your clothes stay cleaner.

The best garden aprons run $15-25 and last for years. Spring planting season is the right time to buy because you'll get immediate use out of it through fall. Here are the options worth considering, from simple pocket aprons to full tool belts.

## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of a Garden Apron

- Load your apron before you start — put your most-used tools in the same pockets every time so you reach automatically without thinking
- Keep a small rag in the front pocket for quick hand wipes so you're not leaving smears on your phone and door handles
- Hang your apron at the door or potting bench, not in a closet — if it's easy to grab you'll wear it, if it requires hunting you won't
- Wash canvas aprons inside out to keep the color from fading as fast
