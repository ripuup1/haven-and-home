If your mattress feels like it is aging faster than you are, a mattress topper is the move that can buy you another two to three years before you need to invest in a full replacement. A good topper adds cushioning where your mattress has compressed, supports pressure points that have started aching, and can even regulate temperature if you tend to sleep hot. The best part is that you can get genuinely hotel-quality comfort for under $75.

The key is matching the right type of topper to your specific sleep issue. Too firm? Memory foam. Sleep hot? Gel-infused. Want that cloud-like hotel feel? Down alternative. Here is exactly what works at each price point for Queen size.

## Do Gel Memory Foam Toppers Actually Sleep Cooler?

**Yes, a gel-infused memory foam topper ($58 for Queen) genuinely sleeps cooler than standard memory foam. The gel beads dispersed throughout the foam absorb and redistribute body heat, which is a real difference if you tend to wake up sweating or flip your pillow to the cool side all night.**

Standard memory foam has one well-known drawback: it traps heat. The same contouring that makes it so comfortable also creates a cradle around your body that holds warmth. Gel-infused foam addresses this with temperature-regulating gel beads that absorb excess heat and release it gradually. You still get the pressure relief and body-contouring benefits of memory foam, but without waking up at 3 AM feeling like you are sleeping on a heating pad. This 3-inch gel topper also has a ventilated design with small holes punched through the foam to allow airflow, which further helps with heat dissipation. If you and your partner have different temperature preferences, a cooling topper on your side of the bed can solve that disagreement without sleeping in separate rooms.

## Are Egg Crate Mattress Toppers Still Worth Buying?

**An egg crate foam topper ($35 for Queen) is the most affordable way to add pressure relief and is especially good for back pain or post-surgery recovery. The peaked surface distributes weight across more contact points and promotes air circulation better than flat foam.**

Egg crate toppers get dismissed as old-fashioned, but they are still one of the most effective options for targeted pressure relief. The peaked-and-valley design means your body weight gets distributed across hundreds of contact points instead of pressing flat against the surface. This is why hospitals have used this design for decades. The air gaps between the peaks also promote ventilation, so egg crate foam sleeps cooler than solid memory foam. At $35 for a Queen, it is the most budget-friendly topper on this list and a great choice if you are recovering from surgery, dealing with back pain, or just want to extend the life of an aging mattress without spending much. The 3-inch thickness provides substantial cushioning. It does not have the contouring precision of memory foam, but for the price, it genuinely improves sleep quality.

## Quick Tips for Getting the Most from Your Mattress Topper

- **Use a mattress protector on top of your topper.** This keeps it clean, extends its life, and makes washing easier since the protector goes in the machine.
- **Let foam toppers fully expand before sleeping on them.** Most need 24-48 hours out of the box to reach full thickness. Sleeping on them early can create permanent impressions.
- **Rotate your topper every 3-4 months.** Flip it head-to-foot to distribute wear evenly and prevent body impressions from forming in one spot.
- **If your topper slides, use a non-slip rug pad underneath it.** Cut a piece of rug pad to size and lay it between your mattress and topper. Problem solved for about $5.
- **Replace your topper every 2-3 years.** Foam compresses over time and down alternative loses loft. A fresh topper is much cheaper than a new mattress.

Your mattress does not need to be perfect if you have the right topper on it. Match the topper type to your biggest sleep complaint, give it a few nights to adjust, and you will be sleeping better without spending hundreds on a new mattress.
