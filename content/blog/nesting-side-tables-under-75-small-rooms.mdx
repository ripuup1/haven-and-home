If you live in a small living room, you already know the struggle: you need a surface for your coffee cup, your book, and your phone — but you don't have the floor space for a big side table. That's where nesting tables come in.

Nesting side tables are literally designed to solve this problem. They sit together when you're not using them, then pull apart when guests come over or you need an extra surface. Two tables, one footprint. Genius.

The best part? You don't have to spend a lot. There are some genuinely good-looking sets under $75 that don't look cheap, hold up to daily use, and work with just about any style. Here are the ones worth considering.

## Can Two Tables Really Replace One Big One?

**Yes — and then some.** With a nesting set, you get flexibility that a single table can't offer. Pull the smaller one out for a guest, push both together as a makeshift coffee table, or use one on each side of your sofa. The versatility is what makes these a smart buy for small spaces.

The key is finding a set that doesn't feel flimsy. A wobbly table that slides around or tips when you set a drink on it is worse than no table at all. The picks below are all stable enough for real-world use, not just styling photos.

## Quick Tips for Styling Nesting Tables

- **Height matters more than you think.** Try to match the height of your nesting table to the arm of your sofa — drinks are easier to reach and the proportions look better.
- **Pull them apart before you buy.** Check how far apart the two tables need to be to pull them apart in your specific space. Some sets need more room than others.
- **Use the smaller table as a footrest.** When company is not over, the lower table makes a decent footrest if you push it in front of the sofa.
- **Style them together like a vignette.** Stack a few books on the larger table, add a candle on top, and let a trailing plant hang off the side. The pair works harder than any single table.
- **They move.** This is underrated. If you rearrange your room seasonally, nesting tables go wherever you need them.
