Headboards are one of those furniture pieces that can cost anywhere from $150 to $1,500 — and if you're renting, moving soon, or just not ready to commit to a permanent piece, that's a lot of money for something bolted to your wall. Enter headboard pillows: the genius alternative that gives you the look and comfort of a headboard at a fraction of the price, with zero commitment.

Whether you prop up in bed to read, scroll, or watch something before sleep, having a cushioned surface behind you beats leaning against a cold wall. These options range from wall-mounted cushion panels to oversized reading wedges, and they all clock in under $30. Your bedroom is about to look a lot more finished.

## Quick Tips for Headboard Pillows

- Measure the width of your bed first and plan your layout — a full or queen bed needs about 54-60 inches of coverage to look balanced
- If using adhesive panels, apply them to a clean, dry wall and wait 24 hours before leaning against them
- Layer a row of euro shams (24x24 pillows) in front of your headboard pillow for a designer-level bed styling look
- For rental apartments, take a photo of your wall before mounting anything so you can reference any existing damage at move-out
