## Quick Tips for Hosting with a Drink Dispenser

- **Pre-make your drinks the night before** and chill — when guests arrive, you just place the dispenser on the stand and go.
- **Add visual interest with ice and garnishes**: floating citrus slices, fresh mint, cucumber ribbons, or edible flowers make water look like a luxury spa treatment.
- **Place your dispenser at the end of the food table** so drink-getting doesn't create a bottleneck in the middle of the spread.
- **Use a small chalkboard sign** to label what's in each dispenser — it adds a charming detail and prevents the "is this sweet tea or unsweet" conversation.
- **Keep a pitcher of backup drink** in the refrigerator so you can quickly refill without disappearing inside for long.
