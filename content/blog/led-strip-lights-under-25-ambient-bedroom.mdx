## Quick Tips

- Clean the surface before applying adhesive — even light dust reduces adhesive hold significantly
- Run the strip along the back edge of your bed frame rather than at the base of the wall for a floating glow effect that doesn't show the source
- For ceiling installations, use additional mounting clips or channel tracks rather than relying solely on the adhesive in high-heat seasons when the adhesive may soften
- Warm white (2700-3000K) or amber settings are best for evening use — cooler colors stimulate alertness and may interfere with winding down for sleep
- Leave a loop of extra strip behind furniture so you can pull a piece out if you ever need to reposition — having slack makes adjustment much easier
