## Quick Tips for Setting Up a Homework Station That Sticks

- Put the station where homework actually happens — not where you wish it happened. Kitchen table? Set it there.
- Label every compartment for the first two weeks until the system becomes automatic.
- Add a weekly Sunday reset to refill supplies and file papers. Ten minutes prevents the whole thing from unraveling.
- One caddy per kid if they have very different supply needs. Shared stations work better for shared supplies like tape and scissors.
- Keep the charging station plugged in and assigned — no borrowing cables.

The 20-minute nightly scramble is a solved problem. Pick two or three of these and set up the system this weekend. By Monday night you'll wonder why it took this long.
