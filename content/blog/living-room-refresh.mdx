Here's something I've learned after years of obsessing over home decor: you don't actually need to buy new furniture to make a room feel completely different. Seriously. The living room that's been feeling "blah" for months? It probably doesn't need a new couch or a paint job. It needs *accessories*.

Think about it like getting dressed. The same basic jeans-and-tee outfit looks totally different when you add the right jacket, jewelry, and bag. Your living room works the same way. The bones are fine — it's the finishing touches that create the vibe.

So here are six Amazon finds that together cost around $100, and they'll make your living room feel like you hired a decorator. Or at the very least, like you have your life together. Which, honestly, is the same thing.

## 1. Textured Throw Pillow Covers

Pillow covers are the single fastest living room upgrade on the planet. You don't even need new pillows — just new covers. Swap out whatever you have for something with texture: think linen, boucle, or a chunky woven fabric. Stick to a palette of two or three complementary colors and mix different textures for that effortlessly layered look.
