## Quick Tips for Using a Towel Warmer

- **Turn it on before your shower, not after** — most bucket warmers need 10-15 minutes to fully heat your towel
- **Don't overstuff a bucket warmer** — one towel draped loosely heats better than two towels crammed in
- **For rack warmers, fold towels in thirds** — this gives maximum contact with the heated bars
- **In humid bathrooms, a rack warmer doubles as a dehumidifier** — keeping towels warm means they dry faster and don't develop mildew
