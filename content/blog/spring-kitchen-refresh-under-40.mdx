New dish towels are the fastest way to bring a spring color palette into your kitchen. Swap out the dark or holiday-themed towels for a set in soft sage, cream, or light terracotta. Linen towels specifically have a slightly textured, organic look that feels elevated without being fussy. Hang one from the oven handle and fold one on the counter near the sink.

## The Total Cost of a Spring Kitchen Refresh

All seven swaps together come in under $135. But you don't need to do them all at once. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do you refresh a kitchen on a budget for spring?

Start with new dish towels and a soap dispenser (under $26 total). These two swaps change the color palette and feel of your kitchen sink area immediately. Then add a fresh herb planter ($18) and updated canisters ($28) for a complete spring kitchen refresh under $75.

### What are the easiest spring kitchen decor ideas?

The easiest spring kitchen updates are swapping dish towels for lighter spring colors, adding a windowsill herb planter, and switching to a citrus-scented dish soap. Each takes under five minutes and costs less than $20. Together they make the whole room feel lighter and fresher.

### How often should you update kitchen accessories?

Twice a year is plenty. Do a spring refresh (lighter colors, fresh scents, live plants) and a fall refresh (warmer tones, cozy textures). You don't need to replace everything. Just swap out the visible items like towels, soap dispensers, and countertop accessories to match the season.
