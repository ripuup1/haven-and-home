## Quick Tips for Getting the Most From Your Bathroom Scale

- **Weigh yourself at the same time every day.** Body weight naturally fluctuates by 2 to 5 lbs throughout the day based on water, food, and clothing. Morning, before eating and after using the bathroom, gives the most consistent baseline.
- **Place it on a hard, flat surface.** Bathroom scales on carpet or uneven tile will read inaccurately. If your bathroom floor is textured, test a few spots to find where it sits most level.
- **Check the surface of the glass monthly.** Tempered glass scales can accumulate soap residue and product buildup from bathroom spray. A quick wipe with a damp cloth keeps the surface looking clean and prevents the sensors underneath from getting moisture damage.
- **Do not store it in the shower.** Even water-resistant scales are not meant for direct spray. Keep it on the floor outside the shower where it stays dry.
- **Ignore day-to-day fluctuations.** If you have a smart scale with trend tracking, look at weekly averages rather than daily numbers. Daily fluctuations are noise; the weekly trend is signal.
