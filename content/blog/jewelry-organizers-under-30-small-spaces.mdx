## Quick Tips for Jewelry Organization in Small Spaces

- Go vertical with wall-mount organizers to free up dresser and counter space entirely.
- Keep your daily-wear pieces separate from your full collection so you're not digging through everything each morning.
- Use a travel case as your everyday organizer if your collection is small. It works just as well at home.
- Store silver jewelry in anti-tarnish pouches or with anti-tarnish strips if your organizer doesn't have lined compartments.
- Edit your collection yearly. If you haven't worn it in a year, donate or sell it. Less jewelry means easier organization.
