## Quick Tips

- Route all cables before sliding the TV stand into its final position against the wall
- Use velcro cable ties inside the stand to bundle cords even after routing through holes
- For floating consoles, always mount into wall studs and use a level
- Leave at least 2 inches of space behind any console for air circulation around electronics
