## Quick Tips

- Use adhesive pegboards for rentals and drill-mounted for permanent installations
- Group similar items together on the board for a tidier, more functional layout
- Leave some empty hooks so you have room to add items without reorganizing
- Plastic pegboards work for lightweight items, metal for anything over 5 pounds
