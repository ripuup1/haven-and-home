## Quick Tips for Choosing an Entryway Shoe Bench

- **Measure your entryway width and depth first.** Leave at least 30 inches of clearance in front of the bench for comfortable access and foot traffic.
- **Count your household's daily shoes.** Multiply by 1.5 to account for seasonal rotation — that's your minimum storage capacity.
- **Bench height matters.** 17-19 inches is comfortable sitting height for most adults. Lower than 16 inches and it's hard to stand back up.
- **Consider boot storage.** If you need to store tall boots, look for open shelves with at least 10 inches of vertical clearance, or a flip-top with a deep interior.
- **Anchor it to the wall if possible.** Especially with flip-top designs, the bench can tip backward when the lid is open and someone pushes on it. Wall anchoring hardware is usually included.
