## Quick Tips for Organizing Pool Gear This Summer

- **Deflate and roll floats before storing** — they take up dramatically less space and last longer when not stretched out all season.
- **Use a mesh bin over a solid bin** — wet gear needs to breathe. Solid bins trap moisture and create mildew.
- **Keep a separate small basket for sunscreen, goggles, and water shoes** right next to the float storage — having one "pool kit" spot means you're always ready in five minutes.
- **Wall mounts work great for noodles**, which are awkward to store in bins.
- **Label zones** if you have kids — "put floats here" removes all ambiguity and actually works.

An organized pool area makes summer feel more relaxed and spontaneous. When the gear is easy to grab and easy to put away, the pool gets used more. Found something you love? Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
