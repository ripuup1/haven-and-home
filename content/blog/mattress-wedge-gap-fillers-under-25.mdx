That gap between your mattress and headboard is one of those small annoyances that bothers you every single day. Pillows slide down into it at night. Your phone disappears into the void. The fitted sheet pulls loose. And yet most people just live with it for years because it does not seem like a problem worth solving.

It is, and the solution costs less than a pizza. Mattress wedge fillers are foam inserts that sit in the gap and create a seamless surface from headboard to mattress. Once it is in place, you forget it exists — which is exactly the point. No more fishing for your phone at 3 AM, no more pillow rescue missions.

All five of these are under $25 and fit standard bed frames. Here is what actually works.

## Quick Tips

- Measure your gap before ordering — gaps wider than 6-7 inches may need a wider wedge or a different solution
- Let memory foam fillers expand for 24 hours out of the packaging before judging the fit
- Tuck your fitted sheet over the wedge filler to keep it locked in place and invisible
- If your gap changes size (like with an adjustable bed frame), choose a compressible memory foam filler that adapts to different widths
