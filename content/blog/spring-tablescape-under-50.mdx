The secret to a good spring tablescape is in the combination: a runner that anchors the look, place settings that coordinate without matching perfectly, and a centerpiece that brings in color and height. ## Quick Tips for Spring Tablescapes

- Layer placemats, charger plates, and dinner plates for a polished look — each layer adds depth and visual interest
- Use odd numbers for centerpieces — one vase or three small items always looks more natural than two or four
- Fold napkins simply — a diagonal fold with the napkin ring at the corner is elegant and takes thirty seconds
- Do not match every element exactly — coordinating is more sophisticated than perfectly matching, and gives your table a curated rather than store-display feel
