## Quick Tips for Getting the Most from Your Electric Kettle

- Descale your kettle every 1-2 months with a 50/50 mix of white vinegar and water — boil, let sit 30 minutes, then boil with clean water twice
- If you drink multiple types of tea, a variable-temperature kettle like the Cuisinart PerfecTemp genuinely improves the flavor of delicate green and white teas
- For pour-over coffee, water temperature matters: 195-205F extracts the best flavor without bitterness — slightly off boil is ideal
- Keep the base cord-free for clean serving — pour into your mug, cup, or teapot without untangling anything
