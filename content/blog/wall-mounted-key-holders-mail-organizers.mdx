## Do Adhesive Key Holders Actually Hold Up Over Time?

**Good adhesive key holders use industrial-strength strips and can hold 10-15 lbs reliably — but only on smooth, painted drywall, tile, or glass. On textured walls, adhesive versions often fail within weeks. When in doubt, use screws.**

Most wall-mounted organizers come with both adhesive strips and screw hardware. Use the screws whenever you can — they take two minutes longer to install and last indefinitely.

## Quick Tips for Entryway Organization

- Mount your organizer at eye level — roughly 60 to 65 inches from the floor — so it's easy to reach and easy to see
- Put the key hook directly beside where you walk in, not across the room, or you will not build the habit of using it
- Designate one mail pocket strictly as outgoing mail to prevent the pile-up problem from starting again
- Add a small basket or tray below the organizer for larger items like sunglasses, dog leashes, or wallets
