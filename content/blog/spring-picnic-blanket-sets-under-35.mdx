The difference between a good picnic and a miserable one usually comes down to two things: what you're sitting on and whether you thought about it ahead of time. A dedicated picnic blanket that lives in your car or a hall closet means you say yes to spontaneous park afternoons, beach walks, and outdoor concerts without the last-minute scramble of grabbing a beach towel that's still damp from last week.

Spring is perfect for this — the ground is damp enough that you absolutely want a waterproof base layer, but warm enough to actually want to be outside. These are the blankets worth buying, all under $35, with honest notes on what works for what situation.

## Quick Tips for Better Picnics This Spring

- Keep a picnic blanket in your car year-round — you'll use it more than you expect when it's already there
- Pair a waterproof mat with a beautiful throw blanket if you want both function and style
- Corner stakes for windy days are worth the 30 seconds it takes to use them — nothing ruins a picnic spread like a blanket that keeps folding over
- A lightweight blanket stays clean longer if you designate one side as shoes-off only from the start

The right blanket makes every outdoor plan feel more doable, not just more comfortable. Found something you love? Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
