Look up. Right now, above your refrigerator, there's probably a dusty wasteland of nothing, or maybe a graveyard of random items you shoved up there and forgot about. That space above the fridge is one of the most underused spots in any kitchen, and the reason is simple: it's hard to reach and nothing up there looks organized. The fix is equally simple. A good basket turns that dead zone into legit storage that actually looks intentional.

The right basket should be wide enough to span most of the fridge top, tall enough to hold items without them falling over, and attractive enough that you don't cringe every time you look up. I've found six options under $30 that check all three boxes. Whether you prefer woven natural fibers, clean wire, or fabric bins, there's something here that'll make that space work for you.

## Quick Tips

- Measure the top of your fridge before buying. Leave at least 2 inches of clearance between the basket and your upper cabinets so you can actually pull the basket out.
- Wipe down the top of the fridge before placing baskets. That space collects a surprising layer of grease and dust.
- Store items you use monthly, not daily. The above-fridge spot works best for backup supplies, seasonal items, or entertaining pieces you don't need every week.
