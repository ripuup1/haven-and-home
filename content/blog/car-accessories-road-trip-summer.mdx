## How to Put It All Together

Treat your car like a space you design, not just something you drive. Start with the phone mount and sunshade (total: $34 for both) because those affect every single drive. Add the gap filler next — it takes 30 seconds and you'll immediately stop losing things. The trunk organizer is the road trip essential; load it before you leave and use it like a moving pantry.

The backseat organizer and the vacuum are the difference between "we planned this trip" and "we threw everything in the car and hoped." At $24 and $22, they're both cheaper than one fast food stop on the road.

## Quick Tips

- Keep a small zip bag with a few non-perishable snacks in the trunk organizer at all times so you always have an emergency snack on a long drive
- Charge your car vacuum in the cup holder while you drive so it's always ready
- The magnetic phone mount plate installs in under 60 seconds — do it before you need it, not while trying to navigate a construction zone
- For kids: fill the backseat organizer pockets the night before a long drive so they wake up and immediately have access to their things
- A clean car is easier to keep clean — start the road trip season right
