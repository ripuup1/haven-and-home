## Quick Tips for Hanging Floral Curtains

- **Hang the rod higher than the window frame.** Mount your curtain rod 4 to 6 in. above the window frame — or even closer to the ceiling — and let curtains hang to the floor. This makes every window look taller and more dramatic.
- **Go wider than the window.** Extend the rod 6 to 12 in. beyond the window on each side so the curtains stack off the glass when open. More light, bigger visual presence.
- **Mix sheer and opaque.** Layer a sheer floral panel with a solid linen drape for a look that is layered, intentional, and functional in any light condition.
- **Steam before hanging.** Most curtains arrive with packaging wrinkles. A handheld steamer removes them in minutes and makes a huge difference in how finished the curtains look.
- **Match curtain length to your intent.** Puddle on the floor for a romantic look, kiss the floor for a tailored look, or hover 0.5 in. above for a clean modern feel.
