## Quick Tips for Adult Easter Baskets

- **Use a reusable container** -- a pretty tote bag, a woven basket, or even a nice mixing bowl makes the "basket" part of the gift
- **Add tissue paper or crinkle fill** -- it takes 30 seconds and makes everything look more intentional
- **Mix categories** -- pair a spa item with a snack, a candle with a mug, or a blanket with a book for a well-rounded basket
- **Personalize with one small add-on** -- their favorite candy bar, a mini succulent, or a handwritten card elevates any pre-made set
