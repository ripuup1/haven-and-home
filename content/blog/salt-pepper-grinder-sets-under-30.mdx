## Quick Tips for Salt and Pepper Grinders

- Fill the salt grinder with coarse sea salt or pink Himalayan salt, not fine table salt which can cake and clog the mechanism
- Store grinders upright, not on their sides, to keep the grinding components aligned and dry
- If your grinder gets sticky or stiff, wipe the inside with a dry cloth and let it air out for a day before refilling
- Ceramic mechanisms outlast steel ones significantly when it comes to grinding salt, since salt is corrosive to metal over time
