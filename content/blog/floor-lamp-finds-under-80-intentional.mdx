## Quick Tips

- **Put the lamp in a corner, not the middle of the room.** A corner placement bounces light into the space and makes the room feel larger. Dead center is always awkward.
- **Match the lamp height to what it's lighting.** Reading lamps work best at seated head height (around 60 inches). Ambient lamps can go taller.
- **Warm bulbs (2700K-3000K) for evenings, cool bulbs (4000K+) for work.** Most of these lamps let you swap bulbs — take advantage of that.
- **One floor lamp is usually not enough for a living room.** Pair it with a table lamp or two to create layered lighting instead of one bright spot surrounded by dark corners.
