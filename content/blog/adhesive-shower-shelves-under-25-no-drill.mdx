## Quick Tips for Installing No-Drill Shower Shelves

- **Prep the surface before you apply adhesive.** Wipe the tile with rubbing alcohol and let it dry completely — 10 to 15 minutes. Any soap scum, moisture, or residue dramatically weakens the bond.
- **Apply firm, sustained pressure when mounting.** Hold the shelf firmly against the wall for 30 to 60 seconds after placing it. This presses the adhesive into the micro-texture of the tile surface and creates a much stronger bond.
- **Wait 24 hours before loading.** The adhesive needs a full curing period before it reaches full strength. Mount in the evening and start using it the next morning.
- **Do not mix heavy and light items on the same shelf.** Most adhesive shelves are rated for 6 to 10 lbs. A full shampoo bottle is about 1 lb, so you have plenty of margin — but stacking heavy glass bottles or loofahs packed with product on the same shelf adds up faster than you think.
- **Choose placement before you peel.** Hold the shelf in position and check the height from multiple angles before removing the adhesive backing. Repositioning is difficult once adhesive makes contact.
