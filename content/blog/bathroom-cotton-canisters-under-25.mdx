## Quick Tips for Bathroom Counter Canister Organization

- **Dedicate one canister to one item.** Cotton balls and cotton swabs together look messy and make grabbing what you need harder. One item per container is the rule.
- **Clear is almost always better.** You can see when you are running low without opening the lid, which means you actually refill before you run out.
- **Bamboo or chrome lids work in most bathrooms.** Chrome matches most fixtures; bamboo goes with anything warm-toned or natural.
- **Keep a small label on the bottom.** If you are using matching opaque canisters, a tiny label on the base tells you what is inside without marking up the front.
- **Group your canisters on a small tray.** A simple rectangular tray underneath three matching jars takes them from scattered to styled in about two seconds.
