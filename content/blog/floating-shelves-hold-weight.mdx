## The Installation Rule That Matters Most

Every shelf on this list can handle real weight, but only if you install it correctly. That means going into studs, using a level, and following the included instructions. I know that sounds obvious, but I've seen (and personally experienced) enough shelf failures to know that most of them come down to lazy installation, not bad products. Take the extra fifteen minutes to do it right, and these shelves will stay up for years.
