The trick is finding one that's functional enough to actually hold what you need but pretty enough that it adds to the room rather than looking like organizational tupperware. You want something that makes you smile when you drop your rings into it at night, not something that screams "I bought this at a dollar store." Every tray on this list is under $25, genuinely attractive, and sized right for a nightstand without taking over the whole surface.

Whether you lean toward minimal ceramic, elegant marble, or glam gold accents, there's something here that'll make your nightly routine feel a little more put-together. Let's find your match.

## Quick Tips for Styling a Jewelry Tray on Your Nightstand

- Keep only your daily-wear pieces in the nightstand tray — everything else belongs in a jewelry box or closet organizer so the tray stays useful, not overwhelmed.
- Place the tray on the side of the nightstand closest to where you sleep, so dropping jewelry into it becomes effortless and automatic.
- Layer a small tray on top of a stack of books or inside a larger decorative tray to create visual depth and make the whole nightstand feel curated.
