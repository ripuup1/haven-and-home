## Quick Tips for Organizing Baking Pans

- **Store pans vertically, not stacked.** This is the single biggest change you can make. Vertical storage means you grab one pan without disturbing the rest.
- **Keep daily-use pans closest to the oven.** Your most-used baking sheet should be the easiest to reach. Put rarely used specialty pans in higher cabinets.
- **Nest similar sizes together.** If you have two identical half-sheet pans, they can share one slot. Save individual slots for different-sized items.
