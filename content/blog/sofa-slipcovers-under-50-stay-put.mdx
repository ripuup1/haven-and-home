## Quick Tips for Choosing a Sofa Slipcover

- **Measure your sofa first.** Most slipcovers list a range (like 72-92 inches). Measure from outside arm to outside arm. A cover that's too big will bunch; too small will pull and tear.
- **Wash before first use.** This softens the fabric and pre-shrinks it so you know the real fit before you commit.
- **Use foam tuckers even if they're not included.** You can buy pool noodles, cut them to length, and tuck them into cushion crevices to keep fabric in place. Works just as well as the fancy ones.
- **Dark colors hide more but show lint.** Light colors show stains but hide pet hair. Pick your battle based on your household.
- **Replace every 12-18 months.** At these prices, slipcovers are meant to be seasonal or semi-annual purchases, not permanent solutions.

A good slipcover can buy your sofa another three to five years of life, and at under $50, that's a pretty solid return on investment. Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
