If you've ever opened a kitchen cabinet and had a wine glass tip over and nearly shatter, you already know the struggle. Stemware takes up an absurd amount of vertical space, and half the time the glasses are just clinking against each other waiting for a disaster to happen. There has to be a better way — and there is.

Wine glass racks are one of those small kitchen upgrades that make a genuinely big difference. Whether you hang them under a cabinet, mount them on the wall, or set them right on the counter, they get your glasses organized, easy to grab, and totally out of harm's way. The best part? From sleek black metal hangers to rustic bronze tabletop stands, here are 7 wine glass racks worth adding to your kitchen or bar cart setup.

## Which Style of Wine Glass Rack Works Best for Your Space?

**Under-cabinet hangers are the most space-efficient choice — they use vertical space you're already ignoring.** Countertop stands are better if your cabinets are too full or your cabinets don't have a flat underside to mount to.

Think about how many glasses you actually use on a regular basis. A 3-row under-cabinet hanger holds around 6 glasses. If you entertain often, look for 4-row versions or countertop racks with 6+ hooks.

## Quick Tips for Choosing the Right Wine Glass Rack

- Measure the underside of your cabinet before buying an under-mount rack — most are designed for standard 3/4-in. plywood, but check the thickness
- If you have tall glasses (like Burgundy stems), look for racks with at least 5.5 in. of drop clearance so the bowls don't get crunched
- Under-cabinet racks mount best on solid wood cabinet bottoms, not hollow particleboard — if yours is the latter, go countertop
- Bronze and brushed gold finishes pair beautifully with warm-toned kitchens; stick with matte black for modern or industrial styles
