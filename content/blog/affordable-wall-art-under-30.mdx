### How do you make cheap wall art look expensive?

Group smaller pieces together instead of hanging one tiny print alone. Use consistent frame colors, and hang art at eye level (about 57 inches from the floor to center). A set of three framed abstract prints ($27) hung with 2 inches between each frame looks like a designer arrangement.

### What wall art works in every room?

### How big should wall art be above a couch?

Wall art above a couch should be about two-thirds the width of the sofa. For a single piece, a 24x36 inch canvas ($26) works well. For a grouping, three 12x16 inch framed prints ($27 for the set) create a balanced look when hung with 2 inches between frames.
