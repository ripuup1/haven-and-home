## Quick Tips

- Measure your cube shelf opening before buying — most standard cubbies are 13 inches but some budget shelves run 11 or 12 inches
- Use 2-3 styles of bin in the same neutral palette for a designer look on a $50 budget
- Dedicate at least one bin per shelf unit to the "random stuff" category so everything else stays sorted
- Fabric bins collapse flat — buy a few extra and store the empties inside a closet for when you need them
- Wicker and seagrass bins are trickier to clean — reserve them for items that don't create crumbs or mess

A cube shelf full of mismatched cardboard and random bags is one of the fastest ways to make a tidy room feel cluttered. Eight bins at under $20 each is a $160 fix for a problem that affects every room in the house — and you can start with just two or three without committing to the whole shelf at once.
