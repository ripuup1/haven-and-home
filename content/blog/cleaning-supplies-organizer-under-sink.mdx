## How Do You Hang Spray Bottles Under the Sink?

**Install a stainless steel tension rod ($10, 18,200+ reviews) between the cabinet side walls, about 6 inches from the top. Hang spray bottles by their triggers, which frees up the entire floor of the cabinet. It takes 30 seconds to install and works in cabinets at least 20 inches wide.**

A tension rod inside the cabinet lets you hang spray bottles by their triggers, which frees up the entire floor of the cabinet. Just wedge the rod between the two side walls, about 6 inches from the top, and hang your bottles. It's genius in its simplicity. The rod costs about $10 and takes 30 seconds to install. The only catch is it works best in cabinets that are at least 20 inches wide. Narrower cabinets might not give you enough room for the bottles to hang without touching.
