Here are six holders that actually work with electric toothbrush bodies, not just the skinny manual ones most organizers are designed for.

## Quick Tips

- Measure your electric toothbrush body width before ordering — Oral-B and Sonicare handles have different diameters
- If your holder sits near the sink, choose one with a lid or cover to prevent water splashes on brush heads
- Clean your holder weekly by rinsing under warm water to prevent buildup at the base
- Wall-mounted holders work best on smooth tile or glass surfaces, not textured walls
