## What Size Do You Actually Need for Meal Prep?

**For most people doing meal prep, you want a bag in the 9–12 liter range.** That gives you room for two or three meal prep containers plus a snack or two without becoming a tote that takes up half your chair.

If you're prepping breakfast, lunch, and snacks for a full work day, look for bags with a separate compartment or at least an internal organizer pocket. Single-compartment bags are fine for one meal, but they get chaotic fast when you're stacking multiple containers.

## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Lunch Bag

- Pre-chill your bag in the fridge for 15 minutes before packing — it extends how long food stays cold
- Use a slim ice pack instead of those bulky gel packs; they take up less space and work just as well
- Don't overfill: a packed-tight bag doesn't circulate cold air as efficiently
- Wipe the interior down weekly, even if you think nothing spilled

A good lunch bag pays for itself in about two weeks of not buying lunch out. Pin this for later and grab one before your next Sunday meal prep session!
