## Quick Tips

- Measure your space before ordering. A bistro set needs at least a 4x4-foot area to feel comfortable, plus room to pull chairs out.
- If you're on an apartment balcony, check your weight limit. Most balconies handle bistro sets fine, but it's good to know.
- Cover your set or bring cushions inside during heavy rain. The frames handle weather, but cushions last much longer with a little protection.
