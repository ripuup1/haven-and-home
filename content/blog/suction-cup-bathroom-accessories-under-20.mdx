## Quick Tips for Making Suction Cups Last in Your Rental

- Clean the surface with rubbing alcohol before mounting — any soap residue or dust weakens the seal
- Suction cups work best on smooth, non-porous surfaces like glazed tile, glass, and acrylic
- If a suction cup starts to lose grip, remove it, rinse the cup under warm water, and reattach — oils from the air can weaken the seal over time
- Avoid mounting suction cups on textured tile, painted drywall, or unglazed stone — they need a smooth surface to form a vacuum
- For the strongest hold, press out all air bubbles when mounting and give it a few hours before loading it up
