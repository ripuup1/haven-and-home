## Quick Tips for Choosing a Side Table

- **Measure your sofa arm height first** — your table should be within 2 inches of the arm height for the most comfortable reach
- **C-tables are best for couch workers** — they slide under and give you a stable laptop or eating surface
- **Round tables soften a room** — if your space has a lot of straight lines and sharp corners, a round accent table adds visual balance
- **Don't forget clearance** — leave at least 3 inches between the table edge and any walkway so you're not bumping into it
