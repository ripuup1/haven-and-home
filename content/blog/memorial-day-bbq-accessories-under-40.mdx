## Quick Tips for Memorial Day BBQ Setup

- **Prep the grill the day before** — clean the grates and check propane or charcoal levels so you're not troubleshooting while guests arrive.
- **Set up the drink cooler before guests arrive** — the first thing people want when they show up is something cold in their hand.
- **One game, one seating area, one food table** — three zones keeps the party spread out and prevents the kitchen bottleneck.
- **Hang the string lights before the day of** — it takes 20 minutes and you'll be glad you did it when the sun goes down.
- **Paper plates are fine, cups are not** — plastic solo cups are the one downgrade worth avoiding. Even cheap reusable tumblers look better.
