## Quick Tips for Styling Velvet Throw Pillows

- Always use a pillow insert that is one to two inches larger than your pillow cover. A 19-inch insert in an 18-inch cover creates a full, hotel-like look. The exact-size insert looks flat and sad.
- Mix textures with velvet: linen, boucle, and woven cotton all pair beautifully with velvet for a layered look.
- Odd numbers work better than even. Three or five pillows on a sofa look more natural than two or four.
- Give velvet pillows a quick fluff and pat before guests arrive. Velvet shows the impression of where you were sitting, which is normal — a quick smooth with your hand removes it.
- Wash velvet covers inside out on gentle cycle and let them air dry flat. Never put velvet in a dryer.
