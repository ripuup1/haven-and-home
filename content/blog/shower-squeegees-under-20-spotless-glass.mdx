Soap scum and hard water deposits on shower glass are not a cleaning problem, they are a maintenance problem. If you squeegee the glass for 30 seconds after every shower, those white chalky deposits never get the chance to bond to the glass surface in the first place. A squeegee is the single most effective thing you can do to keep glass shower doors and walls looking spotless between deep cleans.

## Quick Tips for Spotless Shower Glass

- Squeegee from top to bottom in overlapping strokes so water runs down and off the bottom edge
- Do the squeegee pass immediately after the shower when the glass is still warm and water beads easily
- Once a week, spray glass cleaner and squeegee it off to remove any residual soap film the daily swipe missed
- If hard water deposits have already built up, a mixture of white vinegar and water sprayed on and left for 10 minutes before scrubbing will dissolve the mineral buildup

Thirty seconds with a squeegee after every shower genuinely keeps glass doors looking new indefinitely.
