The good news is you do not need a $500 outdoor kitchen island to get that setup. ## Quick Tips for Your BBQ Prep Station

- Position the cart on the side of your dominant hand so you naturally reach for tools
- Keep a roll of paper towels and a spray bottle of sanitizer on the lower shelf
- Use a rimmed baking sheet on the prep surface to contain marinades and juices
- Store a small trash bag on one of the hooks for immediate cleanup of packaging and scraps
- If the cart stays outside, invest in a simple cover or tarp to protect it between uses
