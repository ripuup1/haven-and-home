## Quick Tips for Solar Path Lights

- Install new solar lights in full sun for 2-3 days before expecting full-night performance — the batteries need initial conditioning cycles
- Wipe solar panels with a damp cloth every few weeks to remove dust and bird droppings that reduce charging efficiency
- In winter climates, bring metal stake lights inside during hard freezes — even IP65 lights can crack if water in the housing freezes
- Place lights on the side of the path that faces the sun (typically south or west-facing) for maximum daily charging hours
