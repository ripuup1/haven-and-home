## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of Flameless Candles

- **Match wax color to your decor.** Ivory reads as classic and warm, white reads as modern and clean, and warm cream works in almost every style. Most sets come in ivory by default.
- **Use the timer every time.** The whole point of flameless candles is that they're automatic. Set the 4 or 6-hour timer and you'll never have to think about them.
- **Group them at odd numbers.** Three or five candles together looks more styled than two or four. Interior designers call this the rule of odds and it applies to candle displays too.
- **Put them inside existing holders.** Flameless candles that are slightly smaller than your glass holders or lanterns will glow beautifully once the light diffuses through the glass.
- **Check the battery type before buying.** Most use AA or D batteries. If you go through batteries often, buy rechargeable ones upfront — you'll use them constantly.

Once you switch to flameless candles in your main living spaces, you'll wonder what took you so long. The ambiance is the same, the risk is zero, and you never have to leave the house wondering if you forgot to blow something out. Found something you love? Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
