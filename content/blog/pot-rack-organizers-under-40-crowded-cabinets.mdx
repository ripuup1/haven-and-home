## Quick Tips for Organizing Your Pot Cabinet

- Store lids separately from pots whenever possible so you can access both without lifting the whole stack
- Group pots by size before loading the rack so larger items go in wider compartments
- Keep the pots and pans you use most often at the front of the rack so everyday cooking does not require moving things around
- Wipe dividers down with a damp cloth once a month to prevent grease buildup from splatter
