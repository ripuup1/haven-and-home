## Quick Tips for Hanging Spring Curtains

- **Go longer than you think.** Curtains that touch or slightly pool on the floor make ceilings look higher and rooms look larger. When in doubt, choose the longer length.
- **Hang the rod higher than the window frame.** Mount the rod 4 to 6 inches above the window opening to make windows appear taller.
- **Layer sheers over blackout liners** if you want the spring floral look without losing sleep. The sheer sits on the front rod, blackout on the back.
- **Wash curtains before hanging** to remove any factory chemicals and preshrink the fabric so they hang evenly.
- **Two panels, not one.** A single panel that covers the whole window reads as cost-cutting. Two panels that part in the middle look finished and intentional.
