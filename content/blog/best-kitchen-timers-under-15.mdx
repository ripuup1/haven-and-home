## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of a Kitchen Timer

- Keep your timer near the stove, not in a drawer — you will actually use it every time if it is within arm's reach
- For baking, always set the timer 5 minutes early and check on your dish, then reset if needed — ovens vary and timers save you from an overcooked disaster
- Use a triple-channel timer on any night you cook more than two things — it is genuinely life-changing when everything finishes at the right time
- If you have a loud household, look for timers described as having a 90+ decibel alarm — most basic timers are not loud enough to hear from another room
- A mechanical timer is the better choice for kids doing homework because there is no screen to distract them — just a satisfying tick and a ring when time is up
