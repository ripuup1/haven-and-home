## Quick Tips

- Place the solar panel in direct sunlight for best performance. Even partial shade can cut the runtime in half.
- Clean the solar panel with a damp cloth every few weeks -- dust and pollen build up and reduce charging efficiency.
- Bring lanterns inside during heavy snow or ice storms. They're waterproof, but ice buildup can damage the solar cells.

Solar lanterns are one of the easiest upgrades you can make to your outdoor space -- no electrician, no extension cords, no ongoing cost. Just set them where you want warm light and let the sun do the rest. Found something you love? Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
