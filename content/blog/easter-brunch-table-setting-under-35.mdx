## Quick Tips for a Beautiful Easter Brunch Table

- Set the table the night before so you can enjoy the morning without rushing — linens, napkin rings, and vases can all go out early
- For the centerpiece, choose one dominant flower and repeat it in smaller bud vases at each end of the table rather than one large arrangement in the middle — it feels more intentional
- Use a spring wreath as a table wreath around your centerpiece vases for a layered, abundant look at zero extra cost
- Keep the color palette to three: a neutral (cream or white), a soft color (sage or blush), and one accent (lavender or yellow) — more than three colors on a table starts to look chaotic
- Fold cloth napkins simply into a rectangle rather than an origami swan — clean and modern always beats elaborate folding
