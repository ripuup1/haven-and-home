## Quick Tips for Using a Bag Organizer

- Mount it at eye level inside a cabinet door so you see it every time you open the cabinet
- Keep a separate one near the door for bags you want to reuse on the next grocery run
- Check bag count once a week — refill before you run completely out
- If the dispenser isn't getting used, move it; location matters more than the product itself
- Dedicate one organizer to grocery bags, another to Ziploc bags — they have completely different use patterns
