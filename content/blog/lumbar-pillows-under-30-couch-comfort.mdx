## Quick Tips for Lumbar Pillow Placement

- **Position it at your belt line.** The pillow should fill the curve of your lower back, not sit up between your shoulder blades. If it's at the right height, you'll feel support without having to lean back hard.
- **Firmer is usually better for support.** Soft, squishy pillows feel nice initially but compress flat and stop supporting you. Go firm and let it break in.
- **Match your couch depth.** Deep couches need thicker lumbar pillows (4-5 inches). Shallow couches only need 2-3 inches or you'll feel pushed forward.
