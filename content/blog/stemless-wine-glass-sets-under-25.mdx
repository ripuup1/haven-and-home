Stemless wine glasses solve the tipping problem entirely, fit in the dishwasher without a custom rack, and look modern enough for company without being so precious that you're afraid to actually use them. Here are seven sets under $25 that are genuinely nice to drink from -- not the thick-rimmed, heavy-bottomed kind that feel like drinking from a jar. These all have real wine glass shapes, just without the stem.

## Quick Tips for Choosing Stemless Wine Glasses

- Match the capacity to what you drink most. 15 oz for white, 17-18 oz for all-purpose, 20+ oz for bold reds.
- Dishwasher safe matters more than you think. You'll use glasses you don't have to hand wash.
- Buy more than you think you need. Having extras means breakage doesn't leave you short for guests.
- Thinner rims taste better. If you're choosing between thick and thin rim, always go thin.
- Clear glass is the safest choice for resale and versatility, but colored glass makes a kitchen statement.

The right stemless wine glasses make you want to use them every day, which is exactly the point. No more saving the nice glasses for company. Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
