## Quick Tips for Wire Basket Wall Shelves

- Always check your wall type before mounting — drywall anchors come included with most, but you'll want toggle bolts for heavier loads or plaster walls.
- Mount produce baskets away from direct sunlight and heat sources so fruit lasts longer.
- Use shelf liner or a small cutting board inside flat-bottom baskets to keep smaller items from slipping through the wire gaps.

Wire basket wall shelves are one of those rare home upgrades that actually cost less than a dinner out and make a visible difference the same day you install them.
