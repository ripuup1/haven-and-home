Kitchen organization has a way of collapsing in on itself — you buy a spice rack, fill it up, and a year later you have seventeen jars scattered across three different shelves, a drawer, and the back of the pantry. The problem is usually space, not effort. Most kitchens just don't have a dedicated spice shelf that's actually in a useful location.

Cabinet door spice clips are the solution that most people don't discover until they see someone else's kitchen and ask, "what is that?" You clip strips inside the door of a cabinet near your stove, and your spice jars hang right there — visible, accessible, and completely out of the way. Most systems cost under $20 and they don't require any drilling. Here are the ones worth getting.

## Do Adhesive Spice Clips Actually Hold?

**3M adhesive-backed spice clip strips hold well on clean, smooth cabinet surfaces — painted wood, laminate, and most melamine finishes — as long as the surface is properly prepared before application.**

The key step most people skip is thoroughly cleaning and drying the cabinet surface before pressing the clips on. Adhesive on a slightly greasy cabinet door (and they're always a little greasy near the stove) will eventually fail. Wipe with rubbing alcohol, let dry completely, then press the clips on and wait 24 hours before loading them with jars.

Also worth knowing: most adhesive clip strips work with standard spice jar diameter — roughly 1.5 to 2 in. — which covers the majority of grocery store spice brands. If you buy in bulk or use oversized jars, check the clip diameter before ordering.

## Quick Tips

- Clean your cabinet surface with rubbing alcohol and let it dry completely before applying any adhesive clips — this doubles the holding power.
- Group your spices logically: baking spices on one door, savory cooking spices on another.
- Label the lids (not the fronts) with a marker so you can see what's what when looking down at the jars from above.
- If your spice jars vary a lot in diameter, measure the widest one and check clip compatibility before buying.
