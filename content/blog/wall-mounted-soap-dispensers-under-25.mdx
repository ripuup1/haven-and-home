## Quick Tips for Installing a Wall-Mounted Soap Dispenser

- **Clean the wall surface thoroughly before mounting.** Even a little soap residue or moisture will weaken adhesive strips dramatically. Wipe down with rubbing alcohol and let it dry completely before pressing the adhesive in place.
- **Give adhesive 24 hours before loading.** Most adhesive strips need a full day to cure to full strength. Mount it one evening and fill it the next morning.
- **Choose placement carefully.** Eye height works well for hand soap by the sink, while waist or hip height is more comfortable in the shower. Think about who uses the space most — kids may need it lower.
- **Refill from the top or remove the bottle entirely.** Trying to refill through the pump opening is messy and frustrating. Most dispensers have a top-fill cap; use it.
- **Soap dispensers work equally well for lotion.** If your countertop lotion bottle is also cluttering the space, swap it into a second wall-mounted dispenser and label it with a small tag.
