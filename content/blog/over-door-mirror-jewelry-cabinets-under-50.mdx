## Quick Tips for Mirror Jewelry Cabinets

- Hang necklaces by length to prevent tangling — longest at the back, shortest at the front
- Use the small pockets and drawers for daily-wear pieces you grab most often
- If you have delicate chains that tangle easily, individual small zip bags inside the cabinet keep them separated
- Clean the mirror with a microfiber cloth to avoid streaks — glass cleaner on the cabinet itself is fine, but avoid overspray on the interior fabric
- If your over-door version swings when opening the main door, add a small peel-and-stick hook to hold it flush when not in use
