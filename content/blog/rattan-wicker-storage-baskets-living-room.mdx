## Quick Tips for Using Wicker and Rattan Baskets in a Living Room

- Use a lidded basket for the most-cluttered items: remotes, charging cables, toys. Use open baskets for things you want visible, like throw blankets and books.
- Mix two sizes on the same shelf for a styled, non-uniform look. Three identical baskets in a row looks like a store display.
- Natural fiber baskets expand in humidity and contract in dry air, which is normal. Do not store them in direct sunlight or they will fade and become brittle over time.
- Line a basket with a linen napkin or a thin cotton cloth if you are storing items that could snag on the weave.
- If a basket smells earthy when it arrives, leave it open near a window for a day or two. The smell goes away completely.
