## Quick Tips for a Spring Bathroom Refresh

- **Start with the countertop.** Clearing off the countertop and replacing a few items with a coordinated set immediately makes the whole room feel more intentional.
- **Stick to two to three colors.** Pastel accessories look best when you keep to a cohesive palette — blush and white, or lavender and sage — rather than mixing every spring color at once.
- **Replace your shower curtain liner too.** A new curtain deserves a fresh liner. They are inexpensive and make the whole curtain hang better.
- **Add a small plant or fresh flowers.** A single stem of tulips or a tiny potted succulent next to your new accessories makes the spring vibe complete.
- **Shop in sets.** Coordinated sets almost always look more intentional than individually picked pieces, even if the individual pieces are higher quality.
