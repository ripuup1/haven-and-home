## Quick Tips for Using Linen Sprays Effectively

- Spray from 10 to 12 inches away to avoid wet spots on fabric — closer distances concentrate the spray and can temporarily dampen the surface.
- For the best scent that lasts, spray freshly washed linens before they're fully dry, then let them finish drying with the scent set in.
- Pillow sprays work best when applied 15 to 20 minutes before you get into bed, giving the alcohol base time to evaporate.
- Don't spray directly on silk or delicate fabrics — test on an inside corner first. Most natural fiber fabrics (cotton, linen, wool) handle linen spray without issue.
