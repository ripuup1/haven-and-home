## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of TV Bias Lighting

- **Mount strips on the very back edge of your TV frame** — the closer to the wall surface, the more diffused the glow looks.
- **Choose warm white (2700-3000K) for relaxing evening use** and RGB for movie nights or parties.
- **Clean the back of your TV with isopropyl alcohol** before applying the adhesive backing — dust and oils are the number one reason strips fall off.
- **Start with lower brightness** and work up; bias lighting works best subtle, not blasting.
- **USB ports on most TVs will cut power when the TV turns off**, so your strip automatically shuts off too — no need to remember to unplug it.
