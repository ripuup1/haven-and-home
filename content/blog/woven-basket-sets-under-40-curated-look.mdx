## Quick Tips

- Buy in sets of 3 or more — singles always look awkward, groupings look styled
- Mix materials (seagrass + rope + rattan) within a room for depth, don't match everything
- If you have pets, skip the cotton rope sets on the floor (they unravel under claws)
- Belly baskets ALWAYS need something heavy inside to stand right — empty they slump
- Natural materials get dusty; shake them outside every couple of weeks

Even three under-$35 sets will completely transform a room with clutter. Found something you love? Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
