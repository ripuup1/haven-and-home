The fix is embarrassingly simple: a body pillow that keeps your spine, hips, and knees aligned while you sleep.

Whether you prefer a traditional straight body pillow, a curved design that wraps around you, or memory foam that contours to your shape, there is an option here that will genuinely improve how you sleep.

## Quick Tips for Side Sleepers Using a Body Pillow

- Position the pillow between your knees to keep your hips level — this is the single most important alignment fix
- Your head should still be on your regular pillow; the body pillow supports everything below
- If you toss and turn, a curved or L-shaped pillow stays in place better than a straight one
- Wash your body pillow cover every 1-2 weeks, and air out the pillow itself monthly
- Give a new pillow at least 3-4 nights before deciding — your body needs time to adjust to better alignment
