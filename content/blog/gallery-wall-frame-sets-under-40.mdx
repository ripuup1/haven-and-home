## Quick Tips for Hanging a Gallery Wall

- Always use the paper template trick: trace each frame onto kraft paper, cut them out, and tape them to the wall with painter's tape before committing to nail holes.
- Start with the largest frame in the center of your arrangement and build outward — this anchors the layout and prevents lopsided spacing.
- Keep 2-3 inches between frames for a cohesive look; anything wider starts to feel like individual frames rather than a gallery grouping.
