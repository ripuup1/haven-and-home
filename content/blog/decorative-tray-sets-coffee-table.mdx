## Quick Tips for Styling a Coffee Table Tray

- **Use the rule of three.** Three objects of varying heights create a balanced arrangement. A candle (tall), a small plant (medium), and a decorative object (short) is a classic combination.
- **Stack 2-3 books as a base.** Coffee table books add color, height, and personality. Place a small object on top of the stack for layering.
- **Include one organic element.** A small plant, a sprig of eucalyptus in a bud vase, or even a pinecone adds life and prevents the arrangement from looking too staged.
- **Leave some empty space on the tray.** You do not need to fill every square inch. Negative space makes the objects you chose look more intentional.
- **Change your tray styling seasonally.** Swap the candle scent, switch the plant, or change the books. Small seasonal updates keep your living room feeling fresh without buying new furniture.

A decorative tray transforms your coffee table from a surface that collects clutter into a styled focal point for your living room. Pick the material that matches your space, follow the rule of three, and you will have a coffee table that looks like it belongs in a design magazine.
