## Quick Tips

- Steam or gently iron fringe that arrives crinkled from shipping. Hold a steamer about 6 inches away and let the fibers relax naturally.
- Avoid hanging macramé in direct sunlight for extended periods. The cotton will yellow over time with UV exposure.
- Use a lint roller every few weeks to keep the cotton looking fresh, especially in homes with pets.
