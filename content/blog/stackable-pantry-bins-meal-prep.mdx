## Quick Tips for Pantry and Fridge Bin Systems

- **Group by meal type, not ingredient type.** A "Tuesday pasta night" bin that holds pasta, jarred sauce, and parmesan is more useful than a "pasta" bin and a separate "sauces" bin.
- **Keep daily-use items at eye level.** The bins you reach for every day (snacks, lunch items, grab-and-go) should be at the most accessible shelf height.
- **Label everything from the start.** Unlabeled systems drift back toward chaos within a month. Labels take 10 minutes and save the system permanently.
- **Leave breathing room.** Overfull bins are as hard to navigate as no bins at all. Fill bins to about 75% capacity so items are visible and accessible.
- **Clean out before restocking.** A monthly 10-minute wipe-down of bins while the pantry is low keeps things from getting sticky and cluttered.
