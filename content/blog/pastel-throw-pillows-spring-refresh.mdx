Swapping throw pillows is the fastest way to make your living room feel like a completely different space without spending real renovation money. When spring rolls around, switching from those deep burgundy and forest green winter pillows to lighter, softer pastels instantly makes everything feel brighter and more airy. It's like opening the curtains after a long winter, but for your couch.

The key is finding pillow covers (not whole pillows -- just covers) in colors that work with your existing furniture. Pastels are universally forgiving here. Soft blush works with gray couches, sage green pairs beautifully with beige and cream, and lavender adds a touch of personality without being overwhelming. These six options are all under $32 and will completely change the vibe of your space.

## Quick Tips

- Buy pillow covers, not whole pillows. A set of 18x18 pillow inserts costs about $20 for 4 on Amazon, and you can swap covers seasonally for years.
- Mix patterns with solids. A good ratio is 2 patterned pillows to every 3-4 solid ones. Too many patterns compete with each other.
- Stick to 2-3 colors in the same tonal family. Spring pastels all play nicely together -- mix peach with sage, or blush with lavender, and it'll look cohesive.

Throw pillows might be the lowest-effort, highest-impact home decor move there is. Twenty minutes and under $40 can make your living room feel like a brand-new space for spring. Found something you love? Pin this for later so you don't lose it!
