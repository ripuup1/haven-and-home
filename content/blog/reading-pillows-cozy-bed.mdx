## Quick Tips for Choosing a Reading Pillow

- **Measure your headboard clearance** — some reading pillows are tall enough to block your headboard or get caught against it
- **Shredded foam is adjustable, solid foam is not** — if you're picky about firmness, go with shredded so you can add or remove fill
- **Machine washable covers are non-negotiable** — these pillows live on your bed and absorb everything. You'll want to wash the cover monthly
- **Try it on your couch too** — most reading pillows work great on the sofa, in a reading nook, or even on the floor with kids
- **Give it 48 hours to expand** — compressed foam pillows look flat out of the box but puff up to full size within a day or two
