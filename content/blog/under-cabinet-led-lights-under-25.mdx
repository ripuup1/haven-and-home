## Quick Tips for Installing Under-Cabinet LED Lights

- **Clean the surface before sticking.** Adhesive doesn't bond well to dusty or greasy cabinet bottoms. A quick wipe with a dry cloth (or 70% isopropyl on grease) makes the adhesive hold much longer.
- **Use warm white for ambiance, cool white for task lighting.** 2700K-3000K is cozy and decorative. 5000K-6500K is bright and clinical — great for actually seeing what you're cutting.
- **Run the cord along the cabinet back, not down the front.** Most strips come with clip hardware to run the power cord along the back of the cabinet and drop it behind the appliances — looks much cleaner than a cord hanging down the front.
- **Overlap one inch when connecting linkable strips.** If you're connecting strips end-to-end, a very slight overlap ensures no dark spot between sections.
- **Consider a smart plug for scheduling.** If your strips don't have a timer, a cheap smart plug ($8-10) lets you set them on a schedule through an app.
