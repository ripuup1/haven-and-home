![Gel nail polish bottles styled on a surface](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1604654894610-df63bc536371?w=1200&q=80)

## Why Does At-Home Gel Polish Peel Off So Fast?

**At-home gel polish peels because you're skipping nail prep. A $9 dehydrator and primer set is the difference between gel that lasts 4 days and gel that lasts 14. Push back cuticles, lightly buff the nail surface, apply dehydrator, wait 60 seconds, then apply primer before your base coat.**

I cannot stress this enough. You can buy the most expensive gel polish on the market, and it'll peel off in three days if you skip proper nail prep.

Start with clean, dry nails. Push back your cuticles (don't cut them unless you really know what you're doing). Lightly buff the nail surface with a fine-grit file so the gel has something to grip. Then swipe on dehydrator, let it evaporate for 60 seconds, and follow with primer. This three-minute step is why salon manicures last and home ones don't. Most people skip it.
