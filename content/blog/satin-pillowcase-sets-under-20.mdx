## Quick Tips for Getting the Most from Satin Pillowcases

- **Wash before first use.** New satin pillowcases can feel slightly stiff. A gentle wash cycle softens them significantly.
- **Use a mesh laundry bag.** Satin snags easily. A mesh bag prevents the pillowcase from catching on zippers or other garments in the wash.
- **Cold water only.** Hot water can cause the satin weave to pucker or lose its sheen over time.
- **Double up if you move a lot in your sleep.** Put satin on all your pillows, not just one — otherwise you'll always find yourself on the cotton one at 3 AM.
- **Replace cotton with satin gradually.** Start with one set, see how you sleep, and expand from there. Most people never go back.
