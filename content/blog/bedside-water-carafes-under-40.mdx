## What Makes a Good Bedside Water Carafe?

**The best bedside carafes hold at least 18 ounces, come with a glass that doubles as a lid (keeping dust and moths out), and sit stably on a nightstand without tipping easily.**

The glass-as-lid design is the one detail that separates a functional bedside carafe from a decorative one. It keeps your water clean overnight, which matters more than you might think. Carafes with silicone coasters earn extra points — they protect your nightstand surface from moisture rings. Borosilicate glass resists thermal shock and feels more substantial than thin glass. And for nighttime use, you want something you can pick up and pour confidently even half-asleep.

## Quick Tips for the Perfect Hotel-Style Nightstand

- Fill your carafe with filtered water right before bed — it makes the morning feel more intentional
- A small bamboo or marble tray under the carafe creates a cohesive nightstand vignette
- Add a single flower bud, a small candle, and your carafe and you have a genuinely beautiful bedside setup for about $30 total
- Guest room carafes make an especially thoughtful touch — fill and set one out whenever you have overnight guests

A bedside water carafe is a small ritual that makes a real difference in how your bedroom feels. Pin this post to come back to when you are ready to give your nightstand a little hotel-style upgrade.
