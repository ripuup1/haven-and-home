## Why Glass Beats Plastic Every Time on a Bathroom Counter

**Glass looks expensive even when it isn't, wipes down in seconds, and doesn't absorb smells or stain when you spill something.**

Plastic organizers get cloudy over time and start to look grimy no matter how often you clean them. Glass stays crystal clear. It also photographs beautifully if you ever want to share your bathroom refresh — not that that's the only reason to choose it, but it doesn't hurt. The weight of glass also keeps jars from tipping when you're digging around in them half-awake in the morning.

## Quick Tips for Styling Apothecary Jars

- Group jars in odd numbers (3 or 5) for the most visually appealing arrangement
- Vary the heights — mix tall, medium, and short for depth
- Keep lids on for a cleaner look if your bathroom gets humid during showers
- Fill partially rather than stuffed to the brim — it looks more intentional
- A small tray underneath corrals the whole grouping and makes it easy to move for cleaning
