## Quick Tips for Setting Up a Magnetic Spice Wall

- Mount a magnetic metal panel ($15-20 on Amazon) inside a cabinet door to create hidden spice storage that keeps counters completely clear
- Fill tins using a small funnel — most spice bags pour messily, but a 3-dollar silicone funnel makes the process quick and clean
- Label tins before filling so you can adjust placement while they are empty and easier to handle
- Put the spices you use daily at eye level and less-used ones higher or lower so your muscle memory kicks in fast
- Wipe down the exterior of tins with a damp cloth every few months to remove grease and keep them looking sharp
