## Quick Tips for Breakfast in Bed

- Use a small silicone mat or grip pad under plates and mugs to keep them from sliding on the tray surface.
- For laptops, a small lap fan underneath the tray improves airflow if you use it for long work sessions.
- If your mattress is very soft, wider-stance legs work better than narrow ones — look for legs that spread outward rather than straight down.
- Wipe bamboo trays with a damp cloth, not submerged in water — prolonged moisture can warp untreated bamboo surfaces over time.
