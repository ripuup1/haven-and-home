## Quick Tips for Choosing a Kitchen Trash Can

- **13 gallons is the sweet spot** for most households — big enough that you're not changing bags daily, small enough to fit standard kitchen bags
- **Sensor cans are best near prep areas** — when your hands are covered in food, touchless is genuinely useful
- **Step cans last longer than sensor cans** — no batteries, no sensors to malfunction, just a mechanical pedal
- **Soft-close lids are worth the extra cost** — the difference between a lid that slams and one that glides shut is enormous in an open-plan kitchen
