## Quick Tips for Seasonal Storage That Actually Stays Organized

- **Label every bin** — Either with a label maker, masking tape, or a marker on the outside. "Christmas — Ornaments" is infinitely more useful than "Box 3."
- **Color code by holiday** — Red bins for Christmas, orange for fall, pastels for spring. Pull the right bins without digging through everything.
- **Wrap lights while putting them away, not after** — The second you leave them in a pile "for just a minute" is the second they get tangled.
- **One bin per type** — Ornaments in one box, lights in another, garlands in a third. Mixing is how chaos happens.
- **Check fragile items before storing** — If something broke this year, throw it away now rather than discovering it again next December.

Future-you will be so relieved when the bins come out next season and everything is exactly where it's supposed to be. Pin this for later!
