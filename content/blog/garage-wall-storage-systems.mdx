If you can't park your car in your garage because every square foot of floor space is covered in stuff, the answer isn't a bigger garage. It's walls. Most garage walls are completely bare while the floor looks like a yard sale that never ended. Moving tools, bikes, ladders, and garden equipment onto the walls is the single most effective thing you can do to reclaim your garage.

The wall storage market ranges from flimsy dollar store hooks to professional-grade systems that cost more than the stuff you're hanging. These picks all land under $50 and are sturdy enough for real-world garage use, not just lightweight decorations.
