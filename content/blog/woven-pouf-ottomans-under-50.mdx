## Quick Tips for Pouf Ottoman Styling

- **Layer your textures:** A knit pouf looks best alongside a jute rug and linen upholstery — mix different natural textures for a curated feel.
- **Add a tray for function:** Place a small round tray on top to turn your pouf into a side table for drinks or remotes.
- **Go for pre-filled:** Poufs that arrive pre-filled are almost always worth the slightly higher price — you skip the stuffing hassle and they hold their shape better.
- **Use two for symmetry:** A pair of matching poufs flanking a coffee table looks intentional and gives you flexible seating for movie nights.
- **Store vertically:** When not in use, a pouf stands upright in a corner or inside a large woven basket for a tidy look.
