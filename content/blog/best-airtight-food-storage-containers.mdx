## What About Flour and Sugar Canisters?

Flour and sugar need large canisters with wide mouths because you scoop directly from them. A narrow opening means you're fighting to get a measuring cup in and out. This set of 4 large canisters fits 5 lbs of flour or sugar each, and the wide mouth fits a 1-cup measuring scoop without spilling.

The airtight seal is critical for flour especially, because flour absorbs moisture and odors from the air. If your flour has ever tasted stale, it's because it wasn't sealed properly. These canisters solve that problem completely.

## How to Pick the Right Set Size

Here's a quick guide: a set of 12 glass containers covers a typical kitchen's pantry staples. A set of 24 plastic containers handles meal prep, leftovers, and pantry storage all in one purchase. If you only need to tackle one zone, start with a set of 4 cereal containers or flour canisters for the pantry, and add produce savers for the fridge later.

Buy the same brand across sets whenever possible. Matching lids stack better, and your storage area looks clean and uniform instead of like a container aisle at the store.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the best airtight containers for a pantry?

Glass containers with bamboo lids (set of 12, $35) are the best overall pantry containers. They don't stain or absorb odors, the silicone-sealed bamboo lids keep food fresh, and they look great on open shelves. For maximum space efficiency, stackable plastic sets (set of 24, $30) fit more in less space.

### How many food storage containers do I need?

A set of 12 covers basic pantry staples like flour, sugar, rice, pasta, and spices. A set of 24 handles pantry plus meal prep and leftovers. Start with one set and add specialized containers (cereal dispensers, produce savers) as needed.

### Are glass or plastic food storage containers better?

Glass is better for pantry display and doesn't absorb stains or odors. Plastic is lighter, cheaper, and better for meal prep and transport. The ideal setup uses glass for pantry staples you want to display and plastic for everyday fridge storage and lunches.
