## Quick Tips for Switching to Silicone Bags

- **Ease into it** — Start by replacing the sizes you use most. For most people that's sandwich and gallon.
- **Let them air dry** — Stand them upside down on a dish rack after washing so they dry completely inside.
- **Label with a dry-erase marker** — Write the date directly on the bag for meal prep. Wipes right off.
- **Check the seal before freezing** — Run your finger along the closure and feel for gaps before it goes in the freezer.
- **Don't use with oily foods right away** — Some silicone bags temporarily absorb oils from strongly flavored foods like garlic. It fades after a few washes.

Once you're fully switched over, you'll wonder why you waited. No more crinkling plastic in your drawer, no more guilt throwing away bags after one use. Pin this for later when you're ready to make the swap!
