## Quick Tips for Organizing Your Pot and Lid Cabinet

- Measure your cabinet interior before ordering any organizer. Height, depth, and width all matter — especially for door-mount options.
- Store lids and their matching pots in the same cabinet when possible. The visual pairing means you spend zero time hunting.
- If you have mismatched lids (from different sets), label the underside with a small piece of masking tape so you know which pot each one belongs to.
- Pull-out drawer inserts work even better than free-standing racks for very deep cabinets — everything slides forward to you instead of requiring you to reach in.
- Do a quarterly purge. If a lid has no matching pot or you haven't used a pan in six months, donate both.
