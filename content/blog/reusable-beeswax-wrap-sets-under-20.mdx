## Quick Tips for Using Beeswax Wraps

- Warm the wrap between your hands for 10 seconds before applying. The body heat softens the wax and activates the stickiness.
- Never use beeswax wraps with raw meat or fish. The wraps can't be sanitized with hot water.
- Store wraps flat or loosely rolled in a drawer. Don't fold them tightly when cold, which can crack the wax.
- Refresh wraps by placing them on parchment paper in a 150-degree oven for 2-3 minutes when they lose stickiness.
- Wash with cool water and mild dish soap. Hot water melts the wax coating.
- At end of life (about a year), compost the wrap or use it as a fire starter.
