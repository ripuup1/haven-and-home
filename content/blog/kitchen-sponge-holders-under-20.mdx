## Quick Tips for a Cleaner Sink Area

- Replace your kitchen sponge every 1-2 weeks regardless of how clean it looks — bacteria builds up fast
- Wring your sponge completely dry before placing it in any holder; this doubles how long the sponge lasts
- Opt for a holder with a drip tray or mesh bottom — solid bottoms trap water and grow mildew
- If you have a garbage disposal button or soap dispenser built into your counter, measure your available space before buying a freestanding caddy
- Consider matching your caddy finish to your faucet (chrome, brushed nickel, matte black) for a more polished look

These are small purchases that make a genuinely meaningful difference in how your sink area looks and functions every single day. If you find one you love, save this post to your kitchen organization board — your future self will thank you when they're reaching for a dry, well-placed sponge.
