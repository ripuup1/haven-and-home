## Quick Tips for Getting the Most Out of Spring Fire Pit Nights

- **Check local burn bans before lighting up.** Spring can be dry in many regions, and rules change. A quick online search takes 30 seconds.
- **Keep a bucket of water or sand nearby** — not just for emergencies, but for extinguishing the fire completely before you go inside.
- **Dry hardwood burns cleaner and longer** than softwood. Oak, hickory, and maple are your best bets.
- **Set up seating before dark.** Arranging chairs and getting the fire started is much easier in daylight.
- **Bring a throw blanket** for late nights when the spring air gets cool again.

Spring fire pit season is short — make the most of it. Pin this for later so you know exactly what to grab before your next outdoor evening.
