Ever tried to read in bed without waking up the person next to you? You know the drill — phone flashlight, angled lamp, still too bright. Eventually you just put the book down.

A clip-on reading light solves this in a very specific way that a bedside lamp never can: it puts the light exactly where you need it, at low enough intensity that someone six inches away isn't bothered. The key is finding one that balances brightness (enough to read without straining), warmth (cool blue light at 10pm is working against you), and clip stability (nothing worse than your light slowly drooping toward the page mid-chapter).

All six picks here come in under $25 and clip to a headboard, book cover, or bedside table. Here's what makes each one different.

## 3. Best for Eye Strain: Warm-Tone Amber Book Light

**If you read before bed to wind down, a warm amber light is significantly better than standard LED — it doesn't suppress melatonin the way blue-toned lights do. At $15, this is the budget pick that's also the smart pick.**

The science on blue light and sleep is real enough that it changes which reading light you should buy. Cool white LEDs (the kind that look like daylight) suppress melatonin and push your body toward alertness. Warm amber tones in the 2700K-3000K range have far less impact on your sleep cycle. If you read to relax before bed and you've noticed that you're still wide awake an hour after you put the book down, switch to an amber light and see if it makes a difference.

## Quick Tips for Reading Lights

- Position the light over your shoulder rather than directly above the page to reduce glare
- For nighttime reading, use the lowest brightness setting your eyes are comfortable with
- Warm-toned light (2700K-3000K range) is consistently better for sleep than cool white LED
- Clip lights work on Kindles and tablets the same way they work on headboards
- If your headboard is upholstered, choose a wide-jaw option with rubber lining to avoid marks
- Recharge when you notice any dimming — most last 6-8 weeks with moderate nightly use
