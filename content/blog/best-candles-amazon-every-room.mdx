### What candles don't produce soot?

Soy wax candles with cotton wicks produce the cleanest burn. The Soy Candle Gift Set ($28) burns clean with no black soot. For zero soot, a Candle Warmer Lamp ($32) melts wax from above using a halogen bulb, releasing scent without ever lighting a wick. No smoke, no soot, no fire risk.

### What's the difference between a candle warmer and burning a candle?

A Candle Warmer Lamp ($32) melts wax from above using heat from a halogen bulb, releasing fragrance without flame, smoke, or soot. Candles last much longer this way since wax melts evenly instead of burning away. The trade-off is you lose the flickering flame ambiance.

### How do you prevent candle tunneling?

Let the wax pool all the way to the edges on the first burn, even if it takes 2+ hours. This sets the "memory" for future burns. The WoodWick candle ($22) is especially prone to tunneling if you don't give it enough time on the first burn. Trim wicks to 1/4 inch before each use for an even melt.
