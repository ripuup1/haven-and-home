If you have ever spent three minutes digging through a cabinet full of spice bottles looking for the cumin you know is in there somewhere, a spice drawer is about to change your life. Moving your spices from a cabinet shelf to a drawer insert means every single jar is visible from above. No more reaching behind the paprika to find the turmeric hiding in the back corner.

The key is picking the right insert for your drawer. Standard kitchen drawers are usually 15 to 24 inches wide and 3 to 4 inches deep, but they vary a lot depending on your kitchen. Some inserts are expandable, some are trim-to-fit, and some come in fixed sizes. The good news is that there is a great option at every price point from $15 to $40. Here are the ones actually worth buying.

Bonus: once your spices are organized in a drawer, you will use more of them. Something about seeing every jar at a glance makes you more adventurous with seasoning. Your cooking will legitimately improve.

## Quick Tips for Setting Up a Spice Drawer

- **Measure your drawer depth first.** Most spice jars are 3 to 4 inches tall. If your drawer is less than 3.5 inches deep, some jars will stick up above the drawer line and prevent it from closing.
- **Standardize your jars if possible.** Matching jars look cleaner and fit organizers more predictably. Transfer your spices from mismatched containers into a [uniform jar set](/blog/spice-jar-labels-organization-set).
- **Put your most-used spices closest to the front.** Salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder should be the first things your hand reaches.
- **Alphabetize or group by cuisine.** Either system works. Alphabetical is faster for finding one specific spice. Grouping by cuisine (Italian herbs together, Mexican spices together) is faster when you are cooking a specific dish.
- **Purge expired spices before organizing.** Ground spices lose potency after about 2 to 3 years. If you cannot remember when you bought it, it is probably time to replace it.

A spice drawer insert is one of those small upgrades that makes a noticeable difference every single day. You cook faster, you season better, and you stop buying duplicates of spices you already have but could not find. Pick the insert that fits your drawer and your collection size, and enjoy finally knowing exactly where the smoked paprika is.
