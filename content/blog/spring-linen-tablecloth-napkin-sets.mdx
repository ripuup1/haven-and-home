## Quick Tips for Spring Linen Tablecloth Care

- Wash linen tablecloths in cool or warm water — hot water can cause linen to shrink by several inches the first few washes
- Remove linen from the dryer while still slightly damp and fold or put on the table immediately to avoid deep-set wrinkles
- Linen wrinkles are part of its character — a slightly rumpled linen tablecloth looks intentionally casual, not sloppy
- Store tablecloths rolled around a cardboard tube rather than folded to prevent permanent crease lines
