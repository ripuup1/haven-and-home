## Quick Tips for Using Silicone Stretch Lids

- **Stretch from the center out.** Place the lid centered on the container, press the middle down slightly, then stretch the edges over the rim. This creates a better seal than trying to pull from one side.
- **Let hot food cool before covering.** Silicone lids are heat-resistant, but covering steaming hot food traps moisture and creates condensation. Wait five minutes for better storage.
- **Use the smallest lid that fits.** A snug fit creates more compression and a better seal than a loose oversized lid hanging over the edges.
- **Store them flat in a drawer or hanging.** They stack flat like a little deck of silicone circles, which takes almost no drawer space.
- **Check the fit on cans.** Standard 15 oz cans are the same diameter — one medium lid covers almost every canned good you'll have open in the fridge.
