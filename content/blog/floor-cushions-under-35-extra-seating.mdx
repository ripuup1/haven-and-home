## Quick Tips

- Let vacuum-packed cushions expand for 24-48 hours before judging their thickness
- Stack two cushions for extra height if you find single cushions too close to the floor
- Darker colors hide stains and pet hair better, lighter colors show the tufted texture more
- Store flat in a closet when not in use to keep them from getting dusty
