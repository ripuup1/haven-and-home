## Quick Tips

- Always use the included anti-tip wall anchor, especially with kids or pets in the house
- Put heavier items on the bottom shelves where the shelf is widest and most stable
- Bamboo options handle bathroom humidity better than particleboard
- Mix books, plants, and decorative objects across tiers for a styled look

Ladder shelves are one of the easiest ways to add storage and personality to a room without a big investment.
