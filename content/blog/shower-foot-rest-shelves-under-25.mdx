Every pick here is under $25, installs without drilling, and works on standard tile, fiberglass, and acrylic shower walls. If you've given up on shower foot rests because of bad suction cup experiences, these are worth another look.

## Quick Tips

- For best suction adhesion, clean the wall with rubbing alcohol before mounting — any soap residue significantly reduces suction strength
- Press and hold the suction cup for at least 30 seconds when mounting to allow the seal to fully set
- In cold bathrooms, run warm water over the suction cups before mounting — cold temperatures reduce suction power
- Replace suction cups if you notice any yellowing or hardening of the rubber — degraded suction material holds significantly less weight
- Position your foot rest at knee height for most shaving positions, slightly lower if you prefer a more bent-knee stance
