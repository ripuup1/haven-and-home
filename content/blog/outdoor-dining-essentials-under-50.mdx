## Quick Tips for Outdoor Dining Setup

- **Invest in a dedicated outdoor set** — keeping separate dishes for outside means you never stress about breaking your good plates
- **Weight everything down** — even with a fitted tablecloth, put a centerpiece or candle on the table before setting plates so nothing shifts
- **Bug protection layer cake** — citronella candles close to the table, a fan nearby for air movement, and bug spray as a last resort
- **Bring everything out on a tray** — one trip instead of five saves time and means dinner starts while the food is still hot
- **Clean up before dark** — leftover food outside at night invites critters you don't want meeting
