## Quick Tips for the Perfect Breakfast in Bed

- Prep everything the night before — set out plates, mugs, and the tray so the morning is stress-free
- Use a dish towel under plates to prevent sliding on the bamboo surface
- Keep drinks in mugs with lids or low, wide cups to reduce spill risk
- Add a small vase with a single flower or a handwritten note for the personal touch
- Clean the tray with a damp cloth after use — bamboo warps if you soak it
