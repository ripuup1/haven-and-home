Take a look at the area around your kitchen sink right now. I'll wait. There's probably a sponge sitting in a puddle, a bottle of dish soap that's been there so long it has a ring mark, maybe a scrub brush leaning against the faucet, and some dish towel draped over the handle. This tiny zone is the #1 clutter magnet in most kitchens because it's where you constantly grab and drop things throughout the day.

The fix isn't about being more disciplined about putting things away. It's about giving every item a designated spot that's just as easy to use as tossing it on the counter. Here's what works.
