## Quick Tips

- **Do a purge before you reorganize.** Pull everything out and throw away anything expired. Most people find they are organizing around things they have not used in a year.
- **Put the most-used things at eye level, not the top shelf.** You will actually use what you can see without reaching.
- **Keep one shelf completely empty as a buffer.** A packed medicine cabinet always devolves back into chaos. Empty shelf space is not wasted space.
- **Quarterly reset.** Set a reminder to pull everything out every three months. It takes ten minutes and keeps the system working.
