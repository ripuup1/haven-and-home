## Quick Tips

- Measure your closet shelves before ordering to make sure boxes fit the width and depth
- Standard boxes fit women up to size 10 and men up to size 9; order X-Large for bigger shoes
- Stack no more than 5 high for stability with most brands
- Take a photo of the shoe inside each box for even faster identification
