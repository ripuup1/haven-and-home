## Quick Tips for Spring Wreaths

- **Use a wreath hanger, not a nail** -- over-the-door hangers protect your door finish and make swapping wreaths seasonal easy
- **Check if your door gets direct sun** -- south-facing doors with full afternoon sun will fade artificial flowers faster; look for UV-resistant options
- **Store wreaths in a wreath storage bag** -- a $10 bag keeps them from getting crushed in your closet and extends their life by years
- **Fluff your wreath when it arrives** -- artificial wreaths ship compressed, so spend five minutes bending and arranging the flowers and leaves when you unbox it
